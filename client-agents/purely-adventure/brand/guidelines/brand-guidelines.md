## Content Formatting Standards

### Article Layout
* Strong headline (clear, no clickbait)
* Lead paragraph that hooks with practical value
* Scannable sections with clear subheads
* Bullet points for specs, lists, and quick references
* Images between sections (never walls of text)
* Clear CTA at the end (subscribe, read more, check gear)

### Affiliate Content
* Always labeled: "This article contains affiliate links"
* Recommendations must be genuine
* Include non-affiliate alternatives when relevant
* Never write content solely to push affiliate products

### Sponsored Content
* Clearly marked: "Sponsored by [Brand]" or "Message from Our Sponsor"
* Separated visually from editorial content
* Honest â€” sponsors don't buy our opinion
