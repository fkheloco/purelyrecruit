# Brio Solutions - Recruitment & Staff Augmentation Process

## Overview

Brio Solutions provides staff augmentation services for specialized construction management and project management roles, with particular focus on aviation and infrastructure projects.

## Current Recruiting Needs (As of February 2026)

### Priority Positions

1. **Construction Manager**
   * **Key Requirement:** Aviation/airport experience (highly preferred but not always mandatory)
   * **Alternative:** Strong project lists demonstrating equivalent complex infrastructure work
   * **Special Consideration:** Willingness to discuss project experience for candidates without direct airport background

2. **Resident Engineer**
   * **Status:** Ongoing need
   * **Requirements:** Technical engineering oversight experience
   * **Focus:** Airport construction projects

3. **Project Manager**
   * **Experience:** Complex construction projects
   * **Certifications:** PMP preferred
   * **Industry:** Aviation, infrastructure, or government projects

4. **Contract Administrator**
   * **Status:** Active recruiting
   * **Requirements:** Contract management experience in construction

5. **Project Control Specialist**
   * **Requirements:** Cost control, scheduling, EVM experience
   * **Tools:** Primavera P6, MS Project

6. **Cost Estimator**
   * **Level:** Senior positions (typically Estimator V)
   * **Industry:** Construction/infrastructure

7. **Public Outreach Specialist**
   * **Requirements:** Community engagement, stakeholder communication
   * **Projects:** Large public infrastructure projects

## Recruitment Process Workflow

### 1. Job Requirements Definition
* Work with Ipsita Kothari (VP) to define position requirements
* Determine mandatory vs. preferred qualifications
* Establish aviation/airport experience requirements
* Define project duration and rate parameters

### 2. Candidate Sourcing (Purely Works Partnership)
* **Weekly Sourcing:** Armeen Haroon leads recruitment efforts
* **Platforms Used:** LinkedIn, industry job boards, professional networks
* **Volume:** Typically 10-15 candidates reviewed per position weekly
* **Recruiter Notes:** Detailed notes added to Airtable for each candidate

### 3. Candidate Review Interface
* **Tool:** Airtable-based candidate management system
* **Features:**
  * Resume storage and viewing
  * Recruiter notes and assessment
  * Status tracking (New, Under Review, Shortlisted, Rejected, Hired)
  * Interview scheduling integration

### 4. Candidate Screening
* **Initial Review:** Purely team (Armeen) conducts first-pass screening
* **Client Review:** Ipsita Kothari reviews top candidates
* **Criteria:**
  * Relevant experience (aviation preferred)
  * Project complexity and scope
  * Technical skills and certifications
  * Communication and presentation skills
  * Salary expectations vs. budget

### 5. Shortlisting Process
* Typical shortlist: 2-4 candidates per position
* **High-Priority Indicators:**
  * Direct aviation/airport experience
  * Strong references from similar projects
  * Immediate availability
  * Salary alignment
  * Geographic proximity or willingness to relocate

### 6. Client Interview & Selection
* Brio Solutions conducts direct interviews
* Reference checks performed
* Final selection and offer

### 7. Onboarding & Placement
* Contract finalization
* Project briefing and orientation
* Integration with project team

## Best Practices & Key Learnings

### Position Requirements
* **Flexibility:** Be open to candidates without direct airport experience if project lists demonstrate equivalent complexity
* **Clarity:** Always confirm whether experience is "mandatory" or "highly preferred"
* **Details:** Capture specific project types, software requirements, and certifications upfront

### Candidate Evaluation
* **Recruiter Notes:** Detailed notes from Armeen are crucial for decision-making
* **Resume Quality:** Strong, well-formatted resumes with clear project descriptions perform better
* **Responsiveness:** Candidate response time and communication quality are evaluation factors

### Communication
* **Candidate Names:** Always verify correct spelling (e.g., Marc vs. Mark)
* **Software Skills:** Ask candidates to list specific software tools used
* **Project Details:** Request comprehensive project lists from candidates lacking aviation experience

### Continuous Improvement
* UI/UX adjustments to candidate review interface based on client feedback
* Alphabetical sorting for closed positions in scoring screens
* Regular data quality checks (scraping accuracy, information completeness)
