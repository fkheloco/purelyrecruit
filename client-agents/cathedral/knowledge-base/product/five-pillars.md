# Cathedral Center: Five Core Pillars Architecture

## Overview

Cathedral Center is built on five interconnected pillars that work together to transform how financial advisors identify, evaluate, and integrate growth opportunities. This architecture ensures comprehensive capability delivery from lead identification through long-term partnership success.

---

## Pillar 1: Growth Intelligence Engine

### Purpose

Data-driven lead identification, scoring, and prioritization for both organic (client/referral-based) and inorganic (advisor/firm recruiting/acquisition) growth opportunities.

### Core Capabilities

#### Lead Identification & Enrichment

- **Organic Opportunities:** Prospect clients and referral sources aligned with target market
- **Inorganic Opportunities:** Advisor and firm acquisition targets identified through market analysis
- **Data Enrichment:** Public data sources (SEC ADV, business records, web presence) automatically enhanced with market intelligence

#### Predictive Scoring & Ranking

- **Tier 1 Public Data Scoring:** Automated scoring based on public signals
- **Predictive Analytics:** Machine learning models identifying high-potential targets
- **Dynamic Ranking:** Real-time re-ranking based on engagement and data updates
- **Opportunity Scoring:** Quantified fit and value metrics for prioritization

#### HubSpot-First Integration

- **Native CRM Sync:** Bidirectional data flow with HubSpot
- **Custom Fields:** "Cathedral Score" field for leads and contacts
- **Automated Pipelines:** Score-based pipeline stage progression
- **Filtered Views:** Advisor/user-specific scored lead views
- **Reporting Dashboards:** Real-time scoring and pipeline analytics

#### Engagement Sequencing

- **Personalized Outreach:** AI-suggested messaging and timing based on lead profile
- **Content Recommendations:** Targeted resources and guides matching lead needs
- **Multi-touch Sequences:** Automated outreach sequences triggered by lead scoring
- **Engagement Tracking:** All interactions tracked in HubSpot for context

### Use Cases

1. **Organic Growth:** Identify high-potential client prospects and referral sources
2. **Inorganic Growth - Recruiting:** Identify advisors aligned with firm culture and capability
3. **Inorganic Growth - M&A:** Score and rank potential acquisition targets
4. **Partnership Opportunities:** Identify strategic partner candidates (custodians, platforms, complementary services)

### Data Entities

**Lead Object**
- Lead ID, Name, Email, Phone
- Lead Type (Prospect, Advisor, Firm)
- Company/Firm Name, AUM, Location
- Tier 1 Score, Last Updated, Status
- Engagement History, Source

## Pillar 2: Advisory Assessment & Attraction Tools

### Purpose

Deep evaluation of retail clients (for partnership suitability) and advisors (for recruiting/M&A), generating actionable compatibility insights as engagement vehicles.

### Core Capabilities

#### Retail Client Assessment

**Purpose:** Uncover client needs and align with advisor capabilities

**Assessment Scope:**
- Client financial situation and goals
- Service needs and sophistication
- Engagement preferences
- Willingness to work with advisors
- Referral potential

**Output:**
- Client profile and needs summary
- Advisor/firm alignment assessment
- Service recommendation
- Engagement plan

#### Advisor Assessment (Advisor Compatibility Index)

**Purpose:** Evaluate cultural, operational, and behavioral fit

**Three-Tier Assessment System:**

**Tier 1: Public Data (Automated)**
- Firm details, AUM, compliance history
- Technology stack, regulatory standing
- Service offerings, team structure

**Tier 2: Self-Assessment Questionnaire**
- Operations (30+ inputs)
- Strategy, Behaviors (DAAN), Culture (91 questions total)
- 30-45 minutes completion time

**Tier 3: Expert Deep Dive**
- Financial due diligence (2-4 hours)
- Legal/compliance review
- Integration planning
- Cultural fit interviews

#### DNA Scoring System

**Five Dimensions:**
1. Culture Alignment (0-100)
2. Values Compatibility (0-100)
3. Behavioral Fit (0-100)
4. Needs Matching (0-100)
5. Desires Alignment (0-100)

**Final Score:** 0-100 weighted composite
- Strong (70-100), Opportunistic (50-70), Threatening (30-50), Weak (0-30)

#### Compatibility Reports

**Self-Serve Digital Reports Include:**
- Five DNA Dimension Scores
- Compatibility Recommendation
- Strength/Gap Summary
- Advisor Profile Summary
- Next Steps Recommendation

**Expert-Guided Reports Include:**
- Detailed findings and analysis
- Integration roadmap
- Risk identification and mitigation
- Strategic recommendations
- SWOT analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats)

### Use Cases

1. **M&A Evaluation:** Assess acquisition target compatibility before proceeding
2. **Recruiting:** Evaluate advisor candidate fit and retention likelihood
3. **Partnership Assessment:** Evaluate potential strategic partners or custodial partners
4. **Internal Assessment:** Assess existing advisor/team fit for strategic planning

## Pillar 3: Workflow Automation

### Purpose

Automated orchestration of key processes from initial lead engagement through integration completion, reducing manual effort and improving consistency.

### Core Capabilities

#### Lead Nurturing Automation

- **Personalized Email Sequences:** Triggered by lead score thresholds
- **Content Delivery:** Educational resources matched to lead profile
- **Timing Optimization:** AI-optimized send times and frequencies
- **Engagement Tracking:** Click-through, open rate, and interaction data
- **Smart Re-engagement:** Automatic re-scoring and re-engagement sequencing

#### Recruiting/M&A Process Workflows

**Triggered Workflows:**

1. **Hot Lead Alert**
   - Trigger: Lead score exceeds threshold
   - Action: Immediate notification to user + task creation
   - Outcome: Quick follow-up on high-potential opportunities

2. **Post-Assessment Outreach**
   - Trigger: Tier 2 assessment completion
   - Action: Personalized email with DNA insights
   - Outcome: Engagement and consultation booking

3. **Integration Workflow**
   - Trigger: Tier 3 completion or deal initiation
   - Actions: Schedule interviews, gather documentation, coordinate teams
   - Outcome: Structured integration process

#### Advisor Transition Workflows

- **Onboarding Sequencing:** Pre-launch, launch day, post-launch milestones
- **Training Coordination:** Scheduled training, resource delivery
- **Systems Integration:** Account setup, access provisioning, data migration
- **Stakeholder Communication:** Advisor, staff, client communication coordination

#### Executive/Staff Recruiting

- **Job Matching:** AI matches candidates to open roles
- **Interview Scheduling:** Automated scheduling with stakeholders
- **Assessment Integration:** Behavioral and cultural assessment as part of process
- **Offer Management:** Offer coordination and acceptance tracking

#### Periodic Lead Re-Ranking

- **Weekly Recalculation:** Scores updated based on engagement and new data
- **Flagged Changes:** Alert users to significant score movements
- **Weekly Summary:** Executive dashboard showing top opportunities and movers

### Data Entities

**Workflow Automation Entities:**
- Lead, Assessment Result, Contact
- Communication Log, Task, Pipeline Stage
- Engagement History, Score Update, Notification

## Pillar 4: Executive Enablement

### Purpose

High-touch advisory services empowering C-suite and leadership to execute growth strategy, combining technology insights with human expertise.

### Core Capabilities

#### C-Suite Mastermind Sessions

- **Peer Learning:** SageCrest facilitates discussions with peer firm leaders
- **Best Practice Sharing:** Case studies and successful partnership models
- **Strategic Dialogue:** Growth strategy discussions and planning
- **Market Insights:** Industry trends and competitive analysis
- **Frequency:** Quarterly sessions for active clients

#### Executive Coaching

- **1-on-1 Coaching:** Personalized guidance on growth challenges
- **Leadership Development:** Coaching on communication, team management, vision
- **Accountability Partnership:** Regular check-ins on strategic initiatives
- **Change Management:** Coaching through organizational transitions
- **Duration:** Ongoing engagement (typically 6-12 months)

#### Valuation & Monetization Planning

- **Firm Valuation:** Fair market valuation assessment
- **Value Drivers:** Identification of value creation opportunities
- **Monetization Strategy:** Multiple options for liquidity (M&A, partnership, retained)
- **Deal Structuring:** Terms optimization for partnership or acquisition
- **Tax Planning:** Tax-efficient structure recommendations

#### Exit Strategy Planning

- **Multiple Scenarios:** Modeling of different exit paths
- **Timing Optimization:** Optimal exit timing based on firm value
- **Buyer Identification:** Matching with appropriate buyers
- **Negotiation Support:** Guidance through deal process
- **Transition Planning:** Post-exit leadership and operational transition

#### Succession & Equity Planning

- **Leadership Succession:** Next-generation leader development
- **Equity Structures:** Ownership and incentive plan design
- **Vesting & Buyback:** Equity mechanics and protection
- **Generational Transition:** Founder/successor alignment and planning
- **Governance:** Ownership and decision-making structure

### Delivery Model

**Mix of Software + Human Consulting:**

- **Technology:** Cathedral Center platform provides data and insights
- **Experts:** SageCrest leadership and Cathedral specialists provide guidance
- **Scalability:** Standard offerings plus custom advisory as needed
- **Ongoing:** Retainer-based for sustained engagement
