# Cathedral Center User Journey

## Five-Step User Journey Framework

Cathedral Center guides financial advisors and advisory practices through a five-step journey, from initial identification and attraction through conversion and long-term acceleration.

---

## Step 1: Identification

**Objective:** Identify prospect advisors and practices with growth ambitions and M&A/partnership interest

**SageCrest Activities:**

### Market Identification
- **FINTRX Integration:** Use FINTRX data and analytics to identify potential advisor prospects
  - Filter by firm size, AUM, geographic region, and business model
  - Identify practices open to partnership or M&A
  - Segment by growth stage and opportunity fit
  
- **Other Data Sources:**
  - LinkedIn advisor research and identification
  - Industry databases and advisor registries
  - Referral networks and advisor relationships
  - Conference and event networking
  - Content engagement metrics

### Prospect Qualification
- **Initial Screening:**
  - Verify regulatory standing and licensing
  - Assess basic fit indicators (AUM, practice size, service model)
  - Identify decision-maker contacts
  - Evaluate partnership motivation indicators

### Targeting & Outreach
- **Targeted Outreach:**
  - Develop personalized outreach messaging
  - Lead with value proposition, not sales pressure
  - Leverage podcast, video, and content references
  - Warm introductions when possible

**Timeline:** Ongoing continuous process; 1-2 weeks from identification to initial contact

**Success Metrics:**
- Number of prospects identified
- Percentage engaged with outreach
- Response rate to initial contact
- Progression to Step 2 (Attraction)

---

## Step 2: Attraction

**Objective:** Attract interested advisors through free self-assessment and access to Cathedral Center resources

**SageCrest Activities:**

### Attraction Channels
- **Learning Center:** Offer free educational content on advisor growth, M&A, recruiting
  - Articles on partnership success factors
  - Advisor best practices
  - Case studies and success stories
  - Webinars and interviews

- **Free Self-Assessment:** Offer access to basic Cathedral Center assessment
  - No commitment required
  - Provide preliminary insights into current state
  - Show value of deeper assessment
  - Create engagement hook

- **Content Delivery:**
  - Podcast appearances and interviews
  - Video content (YouTube, LinkedIn, Instagram)
  - Newsletter and email content
  - Social media (LinkedIn, Instagram)

- **Partnership Visibility:**
  - Fintrex partnership for prospect identification
  - Mojo partnership for content distribution
  - Industry event presence and speaking

### Advisor Engagement
- **Self-Service Assessment:** Make it easy and valuable to engage
  - Simple sign-up process (email/password)
  - Intuitive assessment interface
  - Quick results and preliminary insights
  - No pressure to proceed further

- **Learning Opportunity:** Position as professional development
  - Free content access upon sign-up
  - Educational resources around M&A and growth
  - Benchmark data (anonymized, comparative)
  - Advisor community access (future)

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks from initial outreach to self-assessment completion

**Success Metrics:**
- Conversion rate from prospect to self-assessment engagement
- Self-assessment completion rate
- Learning center engagement
- Time spent on free resources
- Email open and click rates

---

## Step 3: Engagement

**Objective:** Move interested advisors into active engagement through valuable consulting conversation

**SageCrest Activities:**

### Consulting Conversation
- **Initial Consultation:** Schedule 30-45 minute consultation call
  - Review Tier 2 self-assessment results
  - Discuss preliminary findings and insights
  - Introduce Cathedral Center value proposition
  - Identify specific areas of growth opportunity
  - Position as consulting guidance, not sales pitch

- **Value Delivery:**
  - Provide strategic insights into advisor's situation
  - Benchmark against industry standards
  - Identify gaps and opportunities
  - Discuss integration possibilities if M&A interested
  - Share relevant success stories and case studies

### Assessment Progression
- **Tier 2 Questionnaire (if not completed):**
  - Full structured questionnaire completion
  - ~90 questions across Operations, Strategy, Behaviors, Culture
  - 30-45 minutes completion time
  - Confidential and secure

- **Preliminary DNA Scoring:**
  - Generate preliminary five-dimension DNA scores
  - Explain scoring methodology and meaning
  - Highlight areas of strong fit and potential challenges
  - Discuss implications for partnership success

### Relationship Building
- **Regular Communication:**
  - Follow-up emails with relevant resources
  - Additional insights and perspective
  - Continued educational value delivery
  - Relationship development and trust building

- **Executive Engagement:**
  - Introduction to relevant SageCrest leadership if appropriate
  - Peer-to-peer advisory conversations
  - Industry insights and perspective sharing

**Timeline:** 4-8 weeks of engagement, potentially overlapping with other steps

**Success Metrics:**
- Consultation booking rate
- Consultation completion rate
- DNA score generation
- Advisor satisfaction and perceived value
- Progression to paid offerings (LOI, Tier 3, etc.)
- Email and communication engagement

---

## Step 4: Conversion

**Objective:** Convert interested advisors into monetized Cathedral Center relationships through premium offerings

**SageCrest Activities:**

### Monetized Offerings
- **Tier 3 Deep Dive Assessment:**
  - Comprehensive financial, legal, operational assessment
  - 2-4 hour expert interviews with SageCrest leadership
  - Final DNA score and detailed assessment report
  - Integration roadmap and strategic recommendations
  - Fee-based engagement (pricing TBD, likely $5,000-$25,000 depending on firm size/complexity)

- **Premium Consulting Services:**
  - Strategic advisory on M&A process and options
  - Partner evaluation and negotiation support
  - Integration planning and execution
  - Ongoing advisory retainer (if applicable)

- **Letter of Intent (LOI) Support:**
  - Pre-populated LOI template based on assessment
  - Terms and conditions guidance
  - Negotiation support and advice

### Transaction Support
- **NDA and Confidentiality Management:**
  - Secure document handling
  - Confidentiality management and tracking
  - Legal documentation and execution

- **Data Integrations:**
  - HubSpot CRM integration for pipeline management
  - Fentric integration for M&A marketplace engagement
  - Workflow management and milestone tracking

### Decision Support
- **Evidence-Based Recommendations:**
  - Clear DNA compatibility scores
  - Risk identification and mitigation strategies
  - Integration feasibility assessment
  - Partnership viability evaluation

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks to initiate Tier 3 engagement; 4-8 weeks to complete and receive report

**Success Metrics:**
- Tier 3 engagement rate (% of engaged advisors)
- Average Tier 3 pricing and revenue
- ROI of Tier 3 engagement vs. cost
- Conversion to actual M&A or partnership
- Client satisfaction with assessments

---

## Step 5: Acceleration

**Objective:** Accelerate advisor growth and partnership success through continued engagement and coaching

**SageCrest Activities:**

### Ongoing Support
- **Integration Coaching:**
  - Guidance on implementing integration roadmap
  - Regular check-ins and progress tracking
  - Problem-solving and obstacle removal
  - Success metrics definition and measurement

- **Self-Guided Learning Modules:**
  - Library of growth and integration content
  - Best practices guides
  - Implementation templates and tools
  - Case studies and peer success stories

- **Coach-Guided Learning:**
  - Scheduled coaching sessions on specific topics
  - Behavioral coaching for cultural integration
  - Leadership coaching around growth and change
  - Ongoing advisory relationship (potentially fee-based)

### Community & Network
- **Advisor Community (Future):**
  - Network of advisors using Cathedral Center
  - Peer learning and sharing
  - Group coaching and collaborative learning
  - Industry benchmarking and comparative analytics

### Continuous Improvement
- **Feedback and Iteration:**
  - Gather feedback on Cathedral Center experience
  - Identify enhancements and new features
  - Share success stories and learnings
  - Advisor advocacy and referral development

### Long-Term Relationship
- **Growth Enablement:**
  - Continued strategic guidance
  - M&A opportunity identification
  - Recruiting and partnership support
  - Market analysis and competitive positioning

- **Platform Evolution:**
  - New assessment tools and methodologies
  - Enhanced analytics and benchmarking
  - Expanded coaching and learning offerings
  - Integration with additional platforms

**Timeline:** Ongoing, potentially 6-12+ months of engagement for active partnerships

**Success Metrics:**
- Advisor retention and continued platform engagement
- Integration success (timeline, cost, cultural fit outcomes)
- Growth acceleration (revenue, AUM, advisor count)
- Referral generation and new prospect introduction
- Net Promoter Score (NPS) and satisfaction
- Lifetime value per advisor relationship

## Engagement Timing & Conversion Points

### Standard Journey Timeline: 8-12 Weeks

**Key Timing Checkpoints:**

1. **Initial Contact to Self-Assessment:** 1-2 weeks
2. **Self-Assessment Completion:** 2-4 weeks from initial contact
3. **Consultation & DNA Review:** 4-8 weeks from initial contact
4. **Tier 3 Decision Point:** 6-8 weeks from initial contact
5. **Tier 3 Completion:** 8-12 weeks from initial contact

### Fast-Track Timeline: 2-3 Weeks

**For advisors already decided on specific M&A target:**

- Initial consultation: Days 1-3
- Rapid Tier 2 completion: Days 3-7
- Immediate Tier 3 initiation: Days 7-14
- Expedited Tier 3 deep dive: Days 14-21

### Conversion Points in Journey

#### Conversion Point 1: Self-Assessment Engagement
**Where:** Step 2 (Attraction) → Step 3 (Engagement)
**Trigger:** Advisor completes Tier 2 self-assessment
**Conversion Success:** 40%+ of prospects completing assessment
**Next Action:** Schedule consultation and review results

#### Conversion Point 2: Consultation Completion
**Where:** Step 3 (Engagement) → Step 4 (Conversion)
**Trigger:** Advisor completes initial consultation with insights review
**Conversion Success:** 80%+ completion rate; 8+/10 satisfaction
**Next Action:** Propose Tier 3 engagement or ongoing coaching

#### Conversion Point 3: Tier 3 Decision
**Where:** Step 4 (Conversion) → Paid Services
**Trigger:** Advisor decides to proceed with Tier 3 deep dive
**Conversion Success:** 30%+ of engaged prospects proceeding to Tier 3
**Revenue Impact:** $5,000-$25,000+ per engagement

#### Conversion Point 4: Paid Services Expansion
**Where:** Step 4 (Conversion) → Expanded Services
**Trigger:** Advisor engages coaching, executive advisory, or follow-up services
**Conversion Success:** 50%+ of Tier 3 customers proceeding to additional services
**Revenue Impact:** Recurring coaching or advisory engagements

## Journey Customization

### Fast-Track Journey (Already Decided on M&A)
**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks
- Rapid progression through assessments
- Focus on Tier 3 deep dive for specific target
- Accelerated decision-making
- Quick transition to integration planning

### Standard Journey (Exploratory, Not Yet Decided)
**Timeline:** 8-12 weeks
- Full progression through steps
- Educational engagement and learning
- Gradual decision-making and comfort building
- Flexibility to pause or diverge

### Long-Term Engagement Journey (Ongoing Advisory)
**Timeline:** 6-24+ months
- Continuous engagement and learning
- Multiple partnership opportunities over time
- Coaching and advisory relationship
- Evolution from assessment to strategic advisory
