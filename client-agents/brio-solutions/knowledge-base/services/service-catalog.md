# Brio Solutions - Service Catalog

## Strategic Overview: Teaming/Subcontractor Model (2026+)

Brio Solutions operates on a teaming and subcontractor partnership model, with PMIS implementation and project controls as the primary service offering. Services are delivered as subcontractor partnerships with prime contractors like HNTB, WSP, Jacobs, HDR, and ARCADIS on major government infrastructure projects.

**Primary Service for Prime Contractors:** Project Management Information Systems (PMIS) Implementation + Project Controls
