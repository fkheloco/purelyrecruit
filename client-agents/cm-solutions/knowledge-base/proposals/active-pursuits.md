## GO/NO-GO EVALUATION CRITERIA

### Scoring Framework

**Factors Evaluated:**
1. **Market Opportunity** (0-20 points) — Market size, growth potential, recurring revenue
2. **Competitive Position** (0-20 points) — CMS competitive advantages, likelihood of winning
3. **Resource Availability** (0-20 points) — Can CMS resource the engagement effectively?
4. **Strategic Fit** (0-20 points) — Alignment with CMS strategy, growth targets, expertise
5. **Financial Viability** (0-20 points) — Profitable engagement, healthy margins, cash flow
6. **Risk Assessment** (0-20 points) — Client stability, scope clarity, timeline feasibility

**Score Interpretation:**
- 85-100: Strong GO — Pursue aggressively
- 70-84: GO — Recommend pursuit with evaluation
- 50-69: HOLD/EVALUATE — Requires more information
- <50: NO-GO — Recommend passing
