# Cathedral Center Assessment Suite

## Overview

Cathedral Center provides a comprehensive suite of five integrated assessment tools designed to evaluate and enable growth across different dimensions of advisory firm operations, culture, and strategy. Each assessment serves a specific purpose in the Cathedral Center value chain and can be used individually or as part of the comprehensive assessment journey.

---

## Five Core Assessment Tools

### 1. Firm Growth Readiness Model

**Purpose:** Measures an advisory firm's preparedness, capability, and organizational readiness for growth—whether organic or inorganic.

**Target User:** Advisory firm leaders evaluating growth capacity and strategy

**Assessment Dimensions:**

- **Strategic Clarity** — Do we have a clear growth vision and strategy?
- **Financial Capacity** — Do we have capital and cash flow to support growth?
- **Operational Infrastructure** — Are our systems and processes ready?
- **Team Capability** — Do we have the talent to execute growth?
- **Cultural Alignment** — Is our culture conducive to growth?
- **Leadership Competency** — Are our leaders prepared to lead growth?
- **Market Position** — Is our market position strong enough to support growth?
- **Financial Resources** — Do we have adequate working capital?

**Output:**

- Growth Readiness Score (0-100)
- Readiness Category (Ready, Partially Ready, Not Ready)
- Capability Gap Assessment
- Action Plan for Readiness Improvement

**Use Cases:**

- Internal strategic planning
- Board and stakeholder assessment
- Determining growth timing
- Identifying capability gaps
- Strategic priority setting

**Timeline:** 45-60 minutes completion

---

### 2. Advisor Compatibility Index

**Purpose:** Evaluates cultural, operational, and behavioral fit when recruiting advisors, evaluating partnership candidates, or assessing M&A targets.

**Target User:** Advisory firms evaluating new advisors, partners, or acquisition targets

**Assessment Dimensions:**

- **Cultural Fit** — Do their values and work style match our culture?
- **Values Alignment** — Do their core principles align with ours?
- **Behavioral Compatibility** — Are their behavioral patterns (DAAN) complementary?
- **Needs Matching** — Do they need what we offer? Do we need what they have?
- **Desires Alignment** — Do they want the same future trajectory?
- **Operational Fit** — Can we integrate operations effectively?
- **Client Base Fit** — Do their clients align with our target market?
- **Growth Philosophy Alignment** — Do we have compatible growth approaches?

**Output:**

- Advisor Compatibility Score (0-100)
- Five DNA Dimension Scores (Culture, Values, Behavior, Needs, Desires)
- Fit Category (Strong, Good, Moderate, Weak, Poor)
- Integration Risk Assessment
- Retention Likelihood Estimate
- Detailed Compatibility Report

**Use Cases:**

- Recruiting new advisors
- Evaluating M&A opportunities
- Partnership assessment
- Team expansion planning
- Retention risk identification

**Timeline:** 30-45 minutes for self-assessment; 2-4 hours for full evaluation with interviews

---

### 3. Client Journey Mapping Tool

**Purpose:** Identifies gaps, inefficiencies, and improvement opportunities in the client experience journey—from discovery through ongoing service.

**Target User:** Advisory firm leaders and client service teams optimizing client experience

**Assessment Dimensions:**

- **Discovery & Onboarding** — How do clients find us and join?
- **Engagement & Communication** — How do we engage clients and deliver communication?
- **Service Delivery** — What is the quality and consistency of service?
- **Value Demonstration** — How effectively do we communicate value?
- **Relationship Building** — How do we deepen client relationships?
- **Proactive Guidance** — How do we provide ongoing strategic guidance?
- **Problem Resolution** — How do we address client issues?
- **Retention & Advocacy** — How do we retain and advocate?

**Output:**

- Client Journey Map (visual process flow)
- Gap Analysis (current state vs. best practice)
- Improvement Opportunity Ranking
- Implementation Roadmap
- Success Metrics by Journey Stage

**Use Cases:**

- Client experience improvement
- Service delivery standardization
- Team training and alignment
- Integration planning for M&A
- Competitive differentiation

**Timeline:** 60-90 minutes completion with internal team

---

### 4. Value Accretion Framework

**Purpose:** Quantifies the specific drivers of firm and advisor value—identifying which capabilities, operational improvements, and growth initiatives create the most value.

**Target User:** Advisory firm owners evaluating acquisition impact and valuations

**Assessment Dimensions:**

- **Revenue Growth Drivers** — What drives revenue growth?
- **Margin Improvement** — What improves profitability?
- **Asset Growth** — What accelerates AUM growth?
- **Team Productivity** — What improves advisor and staff productivity?
- **Client Retention** — What improves client retention?
- **Service Expansion** — What new services create value?
- **Operational Efficiency** — What reduces costs and improves efficiency?
- **Risk Reduction** — What reduces business and compliance risk?

**Output:**

- Value Accretion Model
- Priority Value Driver Ranking
- Financial Impact Modeling (revenue, margin, enterprise value)
- Strategic Initiative Ranking by Value
- 3-5 Year Value Creation Roadmap

**Use Cases:**

- M&A valuation support
- Acquisition integration planning
- Strategic initiative prioritization
- Growth investment ROI analysis
- Business value optimization

**Timeline:** 2-3 hours completion with financial team

---

### 5. Succession & Equity Participation Assessment

**Purpose:** Plans long-term ownership transition, equity participation, and leadership succession—ensuring alignment with firm vision and individual aspirations.

**Target User:** Advisory firm owners planning for succession or equity incentive structures

**Assessment Dimensions:**

- **Succession Timeline** — When does ownership transition occur?
- **Equity Philosophy** — What is the approach to equity distribution?
- **Participation Structure** — How does equity work? Vesting? Buy-in?
- **Leadership Development** — How do we develop future leaders?
- **Incentive Alignment** — How does equity align interests?
- **Generational Planning** — How do we balance founder/successor perspectives?
- **Tax & Legal Structure** — What structure maximizes value?
- **Governance & Decision-Making** — How is ownership and governance structured?

**Output:**

- Succession Timeline & Roadmap
- Equity Structure Recommendation
- Participation Plan (vesting, buy-in, distribution)
- Leadership Development Plan
- Exit/Continuity Scenarios and Modeling
- Implementation Checklist

**Use Cases:**

- Ownership succession planning
- Next-generation leadership development
- Equity incentive structure design
- Exit strategy development
- Generational transition planning

**Timeline:** 3-4 hours completion with ownership team and advisors

## Assessment Suite Integration

### Standalone Use

Each assessment can be used independently to address specific business needs:

- **Growth Readiness** — Before evaluating partnerships or acquisitions
- **Advisor Compatibility** — When evaluating specific opportunities
- **Client Journey** — When improving operations or integrating practices
- **Value Accretion** — When evaluating acquisition impact
- **Succession** — When planning ownership transition

### Integrated Use

The assessments work together as a comprehensive platform:

1. **Growth Readiness** identifies capability gaps
2. **Value Accretion** prioritizes what value to pursue
3. **Advisor Compatibility** evaluates specific opportunities
4. **Succession** plans long-term ownership and leadership
5. **Client Journey** ensures integration success

## Assessment Selection Framework

### When to Use Growth Readiness Model

**Triggering Questions:**
- Are we ready to grow?
- What capabilities do we need for growth?
- Should we grow organically or inorganically?
- What's our growth readiness score vs. competitors?

**Timing:**
- Annual strategic planning
- Before pursuing growth initiatives
- When evaluating growth strategy options

---

### When to Use Advisor Compatibility Index

**Triggering Questions:**
- Should we recruit/acquire this advisor/firm?
- Will this partnership work?
- What's the retention risk?
- How culturally compatible are we?

**Timing:**
- When specific recruiting opportunity arises
- During M&A evaluation
- When partnership is being considered

---

### When to Use Client Journey Mapping Tool

**Triggering Questions:**
- How can we improve client experience?
- Where are the gaps in our service delivery?
- How do we differentiate through service?
- What's our client journey vs. competitors?

**Timing:**
- Quarterly/annual client experience review
- Before and after M&A integration
- When launching new service offerings
- When implementing new systems

---

### When to Use Value Accretion Framework

**Triggering Questions:**
- What creates the most value in our firm?
- Should we acquire X or Y firm?
- What's the ROI of this investment?
- Where should we focus growth efforts?

**Timing:**
- When evaluating specific acquisitions
- During strategic planning
- When analyzing investment ROI
- Before major strategic initiatives

---

### When to Use Succession & Equity Assessment

**Triggering Questions:**
- How do we transition ownership?
- How do we incentivize next generation?
- What's the tax-efficient structure?
- How do we plan for founder departure?

**Timing:**
- When founder/owner nears transition phase
- When designing equity incentive plan

## Assessment Delivery Models

### Self-Administered (Digital)

Assessments accessible via web platform with self-guided completion:

- **Timeframe:** 30-90 minutes depending on assessment
- **Format:** Interactive questionnaire with progress tracking
- **Output:** Digital report with visualizations
- **Cost:** Included in platform access or standard pricing tier

**Best for:** Quick evaluation, internal alignment, preliminary assessment

---

### Consultant-Guided (Expert Support)

Assessments completed with expert consultant guidance:

- **Timeframe:** 2-4 hours depending on assessment
- **Format:** Combination of questionnaire and expert interviews
- **Output:** Comprehensive report with expert interpretation and recommendations
- **Cost:** Premium service tier; $3,000-$8,000 depending on assessment

**Best for:** Deep evaluation, acquisition due diligence, strategic planning

---

### Full Assessment Suite (Comprehensive)

All five assessments delivered as integrated package:

- **Timeframe:** 8-12 weeks for complete journey
- **Format:** Combination of self-administered and expert-guided
- **Output:** Comprehensive strategic advisory package with implementation roadmap
- **Cost:** Premium engagement; $15,000-$35,000 depending on firm size

**Best for:** Complete firm transformation, major M&A, strategic reset

## Data & Reporting

### Assessment Data Management

All assessment data is:

- **Secure:** Encrypted, secure data storage with role-based access
- **Confidential:** Only accessible to authorized firm users
- **Compliant:** Adherent to financial services regulations and privacy laws
- **Trackable:** Version controlled with audit trail

### Report Formats

Assessments provide:

- **Digital Dashboard:** Interactive visualization of results
- **PDF Report:** Printable comprehensive report
- **Executive Summary:** 1-2 page snapshot for leadership
- **Detailed Analysis:** Full findings and recommendations
- **Visual Displays:** Charts, graphs, and comparative benchmarking

### Benchmarking & Comparison

Premium assessments include:

- **Internal Comparison:** Current vs. previous assessment results
- **Peer Benchmarking:** Anonymous comparison to similar firms (coming Phase 3)
- **Best Practice Comparison:** Results vs. industry best practices
- **Competitive Analysis:** Positioning vs. competitor approaches
