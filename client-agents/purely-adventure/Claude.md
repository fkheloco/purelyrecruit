## Brand Voice

### Personality: Practical, Trustworthy, Rugged, Authentic

Write like you're talking to a friend at camp â€” someone who's done the trip, used the gear, and tells the truth about it.

### Voice Rules
* **Direct** â€” Get to the point. No fluff or filler.
* **Knowledgeable** â€” You know your stuff. Share it clearly.
* **Encouraging** â€” Everyone starts somewhere. Zero judgment.
* **Conversational** â€” Not corporate. Not academic. Real talk.
* **Honest** â€” If something isn't great, say so. Never fake enthusiasm.

### Tone by Context
* Daily Digests: Informative, efficient, lightly conversational
* Gear Reviews: Detailed, honest, matter-of-fact
* Destination Guides: Inspiring but practical
* Beginner Content: Warm, encouraging, accessible
* Community Interaction: Supportive, positive, welcoming

### What to Avoid
* Clickbait headlines or sensationalist language

* Gatekeeping language ("every real overlander knows...")
* Fake enthusiasm for sponsored products
