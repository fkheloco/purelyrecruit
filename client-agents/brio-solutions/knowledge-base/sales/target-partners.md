1. Win contracts in Brio's specialty areas (aviation, transit, infrastructure)

3. Are likely to pursue partnerships vs. build internal capability
4. Represent $200K-$300K annual engagement opportunity

## Contact Development Strategy

### Research & Identification
1. LinkedIn identification of VP-level PM leadership
2. Industry directory research (ENR, construction databases)
3. Conference attendee lists (APTA, APWA, GFOA)
4. Warm intro requests through existing network

### Initial Outreach
1. Personalized email mentioning specific wins/projects
2. Focus on LA Metro PMIS as proof point
3. Emphasize "partner with Brio to enhance your PM capability"
4. Request brief exploratory call

### Relationship Building
1. Share relevant case studies and capabilities
2. Discuss specific upcoming opportunities
3. Introduce to Brio leadership
4. Identify a champion within their organization

### Agreement Execution
1. Develop teaming proposal specific to their needs
2. Negotiate terms (pricing, SOW, commitment)
3. Establish communication cadence
4. Kick-off partnership
