## Assessment Framework Core

### Three-Tier System

**Tier 1: Public Data Enrichment (Automated)**
- Pulls firm details, AUM, SEC ADV filings, compliance history
- Analyzes technology stack and operational infrastructure
- Generates automated scoring based on public signals
- No advisor input required; fully automated pipeline

**Tier 2: Structured Questionnaires (Self-Administered)**
- Operations Module (~30+ inputs)
- Strategy Module
- Behaviors Module (DAAN: Driver, Analytical, Amiable, Expressive)
- Culture Module (~91 questions total)
- Takes 30-45 minutes for average advisor

**Tier 3: Deep Dive Interviews (Human-Led)**
- Financial due diligence, legal/compliance review
- Integration planning, cultural fit assessment
- Conducted by SageCrest leadership or trained specialists

### DNA Scoring (Five Dimensions)

1. **Culture Alignment** — Daily work environment and values match
2. **Values Compatibility** — Core business values alignment
3. **Behavioral Fit** — DAAN profile complementarity
4. **Needs Matching** — Do they need what you offer?
5. **Desires Alignment** — Do they want the same trajectory?

## Key Industry Insights

### The Problem Cathedral Solves

Research shows approximately **70% of advisor M&A partnerships fail** within 5 years due to cultural misalignment, unmet integration expectations, and poor fit assessment. Most firms rely on financial metrics alone when evaluating partnerships.

Cathedral Center addresses this by introducing evidence-based DNA assessment—moving beyond "does the math work?" to "will this actually work?"

### Advisor Growth Challenges

Financial advisory firms face two growth challenges:
1. **Organic Growth** — Building advisory practices through new client acquisition, advanced planning, and team expansion
2. **Inorganic Growth** — M&A, advisor recruiting, platform partnerships, practice acquisitions

Most firms excel at neither. Cathedral Center is positioned as the strategic tool for inorganic growth.
