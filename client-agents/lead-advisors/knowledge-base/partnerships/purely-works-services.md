# Purely Works Partnership

## Partnership Overview

LEAD Advisors has partnered with Purely Works, a staff augmentation and development services company, to provide flexible, scalable development resources for building technology infrastructure, AI agents, and operational systems.

**Partnership Start Date:** 2025 (implementation in progress)
**Partnership Model:** Month-to-month engagement with 30-day notice
**Partnership Lead (Purely Works):** David Hirsch
