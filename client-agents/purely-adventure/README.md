## About Purely Adventure

**What:** An AI-powered adventure media platform delivering personalized, curated daily content.

**Categories:** Land (overlanding) · Sea (boating — power & sail) · Air · Travel

**Mission:** Your personalized home for everything adventure — curated daily, built around what you love.

**Model:** Solo founder + AI agents. Content is AI-curated from 50-100+ RSS feeds daily. Monetized through tasteful ads and affiliate links.

---

## How It Works

* AI scans RSS feeds daily across overlanding, boating, and travel sources
* Compiles unique daily digests per category (Land, Sea, Air, Travel)
* Distributes across website, email, Reddit, YouTube, and Instagram
* Founder reviews output daily (30 min) — AI handles everything else
* Content input: drop .MD files into the system, agent publishes everywhere
