# Cathedral Center Continuous Learning System

## Overview

This directory contains the training system for Cathedral Center, including initial training materials, learning resources, and archives of training iterations. The training system is designed to continuously evolve as the product develops, market feedback comes in, and new capabilities emerge.

---

## Training Framework

### Purpose

Cathedral Center's training system serves multiple purposes:

1. **Onboarding New Team Members** — Bring new developers, designers, and marketing team members up to speed on product, strategy, and brand
2. **Continuous Learning** — Keep existing team aligned as product evolves
3. **Knowledge Documentation** — Capture institutional knowledge, decisions, and learnings
4. **Capability Development** — Deepen team expertise in specific domains (assessment, AI, marketing)
5. **Client Education** — Train SageCrest stakeholders and external advisors on Cathedral Center

### Training Audiences

**Internal (Purely Works Team):**
- Developers (backend, frontend, full-stack)
- Designers (UI/UX)
- Project managers and product leads

**Client-Facing (SageCrest):**
- Leadership (Brian, Cara, Matthew)
- Team members (operational, marketing)

**External (Advisors/Prospects):**
- Advisor prospects
- Advisory firm users
- Integration partners

- [ ] What are the three tiers of assessment? (Public data, questionnaire, interviews)
- [ ] What are the five DNA dimensions? (Culture, values, behavior, needs, desires)

- [ ] What is our launch date? (April 1, 2026)

**Brand & Communication:**
- [ ] What is our core value proposition? ("Consulting not sales")
- [ ] What tone should Cathedral Center communications use? (Advisory, educational, empathetic)
- [ ] How do we differentiate from competitors? (Evidence-based, comprehensive, consultative)
- [ ] What are our five brand pillars? (TBD, but includes expertise, trust, clarity)

**Business Model:**
- [ ] What is the revenue model? (SaaS subscription + premium services)
- [ ] What is our launch timeline? (Phase 1-3, Jan-Apr 2026)
- [ ] What is the development budget? ($6,000/month)
- [ ] Who are the key stakeholders? (Brian, Cara, Matthew)

**User Journey:**
- [ ] What are the five user journey steps? (Identification, Attraction, Engagement, Conversion, Acceleration)
- [ ] How long does the journey typically take? (8-12 weeks standard, 2-3 weeks fast-track)
- [ ] What are the conversion points? (Self-assessment → Consultation → Tier 3 → Paid service)
