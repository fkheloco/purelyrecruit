## Day-to-Day Work

**Q: What are my working hours?**

We're flexible. Work when you're most productive, but be available during overlap hours for your pod. The US-Pakistan time zone overlap is particularly important for cross-team collaboration.

**Q: How do I know what to work on?**

Check your tasks in **Linear**. Your pod lead assigns priorities, but you're also expected to proactively pick up work when you see it.

**Q: What if I'm stuck on something?**

Try to solve it yourself for 15-30 minutes. Then ask in your pod's Slack channel. If it's still blocked, escalate to your pod lead. Nobody will judge you for asking for help.

**Q: Can I use AI tools for my work?**

Absolutely ‚Äî we encourage it! Just make sure you review and refine AI output before it goes to a client. AI accelerates, but humans ensure quality.

## Communication

**Q: Should I use Slack or email?**

**Slack** for internal communication (always). **Email** for external/client communication.

**Q: When should I use a Slack channel vs. a DM?**

Default to channels. Others might benefit from the conversation. Use DMs only for truly personal or sensitive topics.

**Q: How quickly should I respond to messages?**

Slack: within a few hours during your working hours. Email: within 24 hours. If someone needs something urgent, they should say so explicitly.

**Q: Do I need to attend all meetings?**

Only the ones relevant to your work. If an agenda is shared beforehand and nothing applies to you, skip it. Meetings are optional unless explicitly required.
