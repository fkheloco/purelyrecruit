# Purely Works Voice & Tone Guidelines

## Brand Voice

Our voice is **warm, collaborative, and forward-moving.** We sound like a trusted partner who's genuinely invested in our clients' success.

### Core Characteristics

**Warm Professionalism**
- Approachable but structured
- Human, not corporate
- Friendly without being casual

**Collaborative Spirit**
- "We" over "I"
- Partnership over hierarchy
- Extension of the client's team, not a vendor

**Direct with Context**
- Get to the point quickly
- Always explain the "why"
- Provide options, not demands

**Forward Momentum**
- Every message moves something forward
- Clear next steps
- Action-oriented

## Language Markers

**Common Phrases:**
- "I was wondering if..."
- "Happy to work around your schedule."
- "Excited about what we can build together."
- "Just wanted to circle back on..."
- "I totally get how it gets sometimes."
- "Nothing beats a face-to-face conversation."
- "Let's align goals and see what fits best."

**Transitions:**
- "Additionally..."
- "In the meantime..."
- "Following that..."

**What to Avoid:**
- "Per our conversation"
- "At your earliest convenience"
- "Please advise"
- "As per" / "Per se"
- "Going forward"
- Long walls of text
- Commands without options
- Forced humor or excessive emojis
