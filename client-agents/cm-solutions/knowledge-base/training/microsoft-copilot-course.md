## COURSE DESCRIPTION

This comprehensive course trains construction management professionals on leveraging Microsoft Copilot and AI-powered tools to enhance project controls, reporting, and operational efficiency. Participants will learn to build custom Copilot agents, integrate AI with their existing CM software (Procore, BIM 360, etc.), and apply advanced AI techniques for project documentation, schedule optimization, and knowledge management.

The course combines foundational AI concepts with practical, hands-on exercises using real construction management scenarios. Graduates will be equipped to drive AI adoption within their organizations and improve project team productivity.
