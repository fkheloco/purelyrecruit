## Use in Teaming Proposals & Partnerships

When developing proposals or partnership discussions with prime contractors, emphasize:

1. **For Mega-Contractors (Tier 1):** "Be the PM expert" - Outsource PMIS and controls
2. **For Regional Contractors (Tier 2-3):** "Compete with larger firms" - Scale capability without overhead
3. **For Design Firms Expanding Services:** "Add PM to your offering" - White-label PMIS and PM services
4. **For Government Agencies (Tier 5):** "Government-grade PMIS" - Compliance, transparency, multi-contractor coordination
5. **For RFP Support:** Leverage AI agents for superior proposal quality and faster turnaround

### Customization by Partner Type

**Tier 1 Mega-Contractors (HNTB, WSP, Jacobs, HDR, ARCADIS)**
* Emphasize enterprise-scale LA Metro implementation
* Position as "outsourced PM team" for large programs
* Highlight competitive advantage on government RFPs
* Focus on recurring revenue and multi-year engagement

**Tier 2-3 Regional Contractors**
* Emphasize cost-effectiveness vs. building internal PM team
* Highlight ability to compete on larger contracts
* Position as scalable PM capability
* Offer flexible engagement models

**Design Firms Entering PM Services**
* Position as white-label PM services provider
* Emphasize "expand your service offering without hiring"
* Highlight PMIS as valuable client deliverable
* Focus on PM-for-Fee opportunities

**Government/Public Agencies**
* Emphasize government compliance and transparency
* Highlight proven LA Metro experience
* Focus on multi-contractor coordination
* Position as independent oversight/controls

Always customize the value proposition to address the specific pain points and requirements mentioned in RFPs or partnership discussions.
