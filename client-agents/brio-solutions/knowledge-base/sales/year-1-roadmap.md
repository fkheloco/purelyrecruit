# Brio Solutions - Year 1 Roadmap (2026)

## Executive Summary

Brio Solutions' year-long roadmap to establish the teaming/subcontractor partnership model as the primary growth engine. The roadmap balances partnership development, AI agent deployment, recruiting pipeline, and revenue generation across four quarters.

**Year 1 Revenue Target:** $700K run-rate by Q4 2026 (Realistic: $400K actual Year 1)
**Teaming Agreements Target:** 8-12 signed agreements
**Active Subcontracts Target:** 3 by Q4 2026
**Success Metric:** 100% partner satisfaction, 50%+ margin on subcontracts

## Risk Management

### Key Risks & Mitigation

**Risk: Partner Receptiveness to Teaming Model**
* **Mitigation:** Compelling value prop, LA Metro case study, executive sponsorship
* **Contingency:** Adjust messaging, focus on specific partner pain points

**Risk: AI Agents Don't Meet Expectations**
* **Mitigation:** Early testing, feedback loop, iterative improvement
* **Contingency:** Fallback to manual processes, refine agent design

**Risk: Recruiting Pipeline Doesn't Materialize**
* **Mitigation:** Diversified sourcing, dedicated recruiting effort, early hiring
* **Contingency:** Contract staffing, expand geographic sourcing

**Risk: First Subcontract Underperforms**
* **Mitigation:** Strong team selection, PMIS planning, executive oversight
* **Contingency:** Additional support, scope adjustment, relationship recovery

**Risk: Market Downturn Impacts Infrastructure Spending**
* **Mitigation:** Diversified partner base, multiple sectors, government focus
* **Contingency:** Extend timeline, adjust targets, emphasize cost savings
