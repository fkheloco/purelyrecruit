# Brio Solutions - Typical Project Types & Client Work

## Aviation & Airport Projects

### Characteristics
* **Clients:** Airport authorities, aviation agencies, contractors
* **Typical Scope:** Terminal construction, airside improvements, infrastructure upgrades
* **Special Requirements:**
  * FAA regulations and compliance
  * 24/7 operational coordination
  * Security clearances
  * Minimal disruption to operations
* **Key Positions Needed:** Construction managers, resident engineers, project control specialists
* **Experience Requirements:** Aviation/airport background strongly preferred

### Example Projects
* Terminal renovations and expansions
* Airfield improvements (runways, taxiways, aprons)
* Baggage handling systems
* Airside infrastructure
* Terminal concessions and retail spaces
