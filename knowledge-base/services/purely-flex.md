# Purely Flex Service Model

## Overview

Purely Flex gives clients flexible access to all three service areas (Recruiting, Proposals, Development) with bandwidth that shifts based on monthly priorities.

**Price:** $6,000/month

## What's Included

### Recruiting Services
* Active support for 1-2 open positions at any time
* AI-powered candidate scoring customized to client culture
* Human recruiters sourcing across Indeed, LinkedIn, specialty platforms
* Detailed candidate profiles with scoring rationale
* Integration with client's existing systems

### Proposal Services
* 1 professionally managed proposal per quarter (4 per year)
* Full coordination, technical writing, and graphic design
* Database organization of past projects and capabilities
* Professional deliverables that meet compliance standards

### Custom Development
* 10-15 hours per week of focused development work
* Flexible across specializations: Full Stack, Data/AI, Systems, Salesforce
* AI automation and workflow improvements
* Integration projects and tool development
* Ongoing system maintenance and enhancements

## The Elasticity Advantage

Priorities shift month to month. Purely Flex adapts with you:

**Example Monthly Flow:**
* **Month 1:** AI readiness assessment and strategic planning
* **Month 2:** Development focus - building workflow automation
* **Month 3:** Recruiting priority - filling critical position while development continues lighter
* **Month 4:** Proposal crunch - major bid requires full attention
* **Month 5:** Back to development for CRM integration

The team adjusts bandwidth based on what's most urgent. Clients aren't locked into rigid allocations.

## Perfect For

* Companies new to working with remote teams who want to test multiple services
* Businesses with fluctuating needs across recruiting, proposals, and development
* Organizations looking for strategic flexibility without overcommitment
* Teams that need consistent support across all three domains at moderate volume

## Scaling From Purely Flex

Clients can always add dedicated capacity when they discover where they need it most:

* **Growing recruiting needs?** Add a Focused Recruiting Team
* **Proposal volume increasing?** Add a Focused Proposal Team
* **Development backlog expanding?** Add development specialists

**Purely Flex becomes the baseline** while focused teams handle high-volume areas.

## Key Benefits

* Builds trust and proves ROI before scaling investment
* No long-term contracts - month-to-month
