# VSCE, Inc. - Company Overview

## Executive Summary

VSCE, Inc. is a specialized civil engineering, architecture, and program management consulting firm serving public-sector transportation agencies and government clients throughout California and the Pacific Northwest. With 20+ years of experience and $2 billion in combined project delivery, VSCE provides strategic program management, capital program analysis, construction management, and procurement consulting services to regional transit authorities, state departments, and municipalities.

## Certifications & Designations

VSCE holds multiple small business and diverse business enterprise certifications critical for public-sector procurement:

### Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (DBE)
- **Certifying Agency:** Caltrans (California Department of Transportation)
- **Status:** Active
- **Relevance:** Qualifies for DBE contract set-asides and goals on state highway and transportation projects
- **Value:** Significant competitive advantage on Caltrans projects

### Small Business Enterprise (SBE)
- **Certifying Agency:** California Department of General Services (CA DGS)
- **Status:** Active
- **Relevance:** Qualifies for state small business procurement preferences
- **Value:** Advantage on state agency contracts

### Minority Business Enterprise (MBE)
- **Certifying Agency:** California Public Utilities Commission (CA PUC)
- **Status:** Active
- **Relevance:** Qualifies for utility and public utility commission projects
- **Value:** Strategic advantage on utility-related infrastructure projects

### Small Local Emerging Business (SLEB)
- **Certifying Agency:** Alameda County
- **Status:** Active
- **Coverage Area:** Alameda County and surrounding Bay Area
- **Value:** Preference on county and local agency contracts

### Local Business Enterprise (LBE)
- **Certifying Agency:** Port of Oakland
- **Status:** Active
- **Relevance:** Preferred vendor status with Port of Oakland
- **Value:** Advantage on port-related projects

### LA Metro Certifications
- **Status:** Active (multiple designations)
- **Designations:** DBE, SB (Small Business), LBE (Local Business Enterprise)
- **Relevance:** Critical for Southern California Metro projects
- **Value:** Competitive advantage on LA Metro procurement

### Strategic Importance
These certifications are not administrative formalities—they are competitive prerequisites for major RFP responses. Many public agencies (especially transportation authorities) have explicit DBE/MBE goals and set-asides. VSCE's multiple certifications across different regulatory bodies allow the company to:
- Bid independently on DBE set-asides
- Serve as DBE partner for larger prime contractors
- Meet agency diversity goals and metrics
- Access specialized funding and contracting opportunities
