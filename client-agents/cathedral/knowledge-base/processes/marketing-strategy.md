# Cathedral Center Marketing Strategy

## Marketing Overview

Cathedral Center's marketing strategy focuses on thought leadership, content-driven engagement, and relationship building with financial advisors. The approach emphasizes "consulting not sales" by positioning Cathedral Center as a valuable advisory resource rather than a transactional tool.

**Core Marketing Principle:** Add value first, build trust, then convert to paid offerings

---

## Marketing Channels & Tactics

### 1. Podcast Content

**Objective:** Build authority and reach advisors through audio content

**Strategy:**
- **Guest Appearances:** Position SageCrest/Cathedral leaders on relevant financial advisor podcasts
  - Target: Podcasts focused on advisor growth, M&A, business management, financial planning
  - Topics: Advisor growth strategies, M&A success, partnership evaluation, cultural fit
  - Frequency: 2-3 appearances per month during launch period

- **Original Podcast:** Consider launching "Cathedral Center Advisor Growth" podcast
  - Format: 30-45 minute episodes, weekly
  - Content: Advisor interviews, growth stories, M&A insights, best practices
  - Launch: Post-MVP, potentially Q2 2026

**Key Messages:**
- "Most advisor partnerships fail due to poor fit, not financial metrics"
- "Evidence-based assessment reduces M&A risk and improves success"
- "Growing inorganically requires strategic evaluation and planning"

**Promotion:**
- Share across social media (LinkedIn, Instagram)
- Include in email campaigns
- Guest post and article follow-ups
- Case study development from episodes

---

### 2. Short-Form Video Content

**Objective:** Reach visual learners and build social media presence

**Platforms:**
- **LinkedIn Video** (primary)
- **Instagram Reels** (secondary)
- **YouTube Shorts** (TBD)

**Content Themes:**

**Series 1: "M&A Mistakes"** (30-60 seconds)
- Common mistakes in advisor M&A
- Why partnerships fail
- Quick tips for better evaluation
- CTA: "Learn the science of advisor fit"

**Series 2: "Advisor Spotlight"** (45-90 seconds)
- Case studies of successful partnerships
- "Here's why this partnership worked"
- Cultural and values alignment stories
- CTA: "Assess your own fit"

**Series 3: "The DNA Score"** (30 seconds)
- Explainers about five DNA dimensions
- Quick facts about culture, values, behavior, needs, desires
- Why each matters for partnerships
- CTA: Link to learning center

**Series 4: "Growth Challenge"** (45 seconds)
- Common advisor growth challenges
- How Cathedral Center addresses them
- Thought leadership positioning
- CTA: "Get expert guidance"

**Production:** 2-3 videos per week, 6-8 week sprint before launch

**Distribution:**
- Post natively on each platform
- Repurpose across channels
- Share in email campaigns
- Use for LinkedIn content strategy

### 3. LinkedIn Strategy

**Objective:** Build professional credibility and reach advisor decision-makers

**Content Mix:**
- **Thought Leadership** (40%): Industry insights, advisory trends, growth strategies
- **Educational** (30%): Assessment tips, partnership success factors, growth planning
- **Social Proof** (20%): Client stories, case studies, success metrics
- **Promotional** (10%): Cathedral Center features, platform updates, webinar invites

**Posting Schedule:**
- 3-5 posts per week
- Best times: Tuesday-Thursday, 9am-12pm PT
- Mix of text, images, videos, links

**Content Themes:**
- Advisor growth and M&A trends
- Partnership and cultural fit insights
- M&A failure statistics and prevention
- Recruiting and talent management
- Leadership and firm culture

**Engagement:**
- Comment on relevant industry posts
- Engage with advisor community discussions
- Participate in relevant LinkedIn Groups
- Share and amplify partner content (Mojo, Fintrex, etc.)

**Key Voice:** Brian Church, SageCrest leadership (authentic, credible advisors sharing expertise)

### 4. Email Marketing

**Objective:** Nurture relationships and guide prospects through journey

**Segmentation:**

**Segment 1: Cold Prospects** (Not yet engaged)
- 5-email welcome series introducing Cathedral Center
- Content: Industry insights, advisor growth trends
- Frequency: 1-2 per week
- CTA: Free self-assessment, learning center access

**Segment 2: Engaged Prospects** (Completed Tier 2 assessment)
- Educational content on assessment insights
- Deep dives on specific DNA dimensions
- Partnership success stories
- Frequency: 1-2 per week
- CTA: Schedule consultation, Tier 3 engagement

**Segment 3: Active Customers** (Completed Tier 3 or ongoing)
- Implementation tips and success stories
- Coaching content and resources
- Platform updates and new features
- Community and network updates
- Frequency: 1 per week
- CTA: Coaching engagement, community participation

**Email Content Types:**

1. **Insight & Thought Leadership**
   - "The Truth About Advisor M&A Success"
   - "Why Cultural Fit Matters More Than AUM"
   - Industry trend analysis and commentary

2. **Educational**
   - "Understanding Your DNA Score"
   - "The DAAN Behavioral Framework Explained"
   - "Integration Planning 101"

3. **Social Proof**
   - Case studies of successful partnerships
   - Client testimonials
   - Success metrics and outcomes

4. **Promotional**
   - Free assessment invitation
   - Consultation booking
   - Limited-time offers (webinars, guides)

**Design & Tone:**
- Professional, conversational, advisory
- Consistent SageCrest branding
- Mobile-optimized
- Clear CTAs (not pushy)
