# LEAD Advisors Marketing Strategy

## Marketing Strategy Overview

LEAD Advisors' marketing strategy represents a transition from primarily outbound, manual lead generation to a more sophisticated, AI-enabled, multi-channel approach. The strategy is organized around current channels (being optimized) and new channels (being built), supported by technology development and implementation.

This document outlines the strategic direction approved by LEAD Advisors leadership and the technology roadmap for implementation.

---

## Current Marketing Channels (Being Optimized)

### Outbound Lead Generation (Current Primary Channel)

**Current Approach:**
- Manual outreach via LinkedIn, email, and phone
- Using ZoomInfo, Apollo, LinkedIn Sales Navigator for prospect identification and research
- Relationship-based and event-based lead generation
- NEPQ methodology for discovery

**Tools & Platforms:**
- **ZoomInfo:** Prospect database, enrichment, and outreach
- **Apollo:** Similar capabilities to ZoomInfo; redundancy and coverage
- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator:** Prospect research and LinkedIn outreach
- **Pipedrive:** CRM for pipeline management
- **Email:** Direct email outreach campaigns

**Effectiveness:**
- Generates consistent pipeline
- Strong conversion rate due to quality targeting
- Time-intensive (manual outreach)
- Dependent on salesperson effort and skill

**Optimization Opportunities:**
- Increase volume of outreach
- Improve email effectiveness (A/B testing, templates)
- Better lead qualification before outreach
- Automation of routine follow-up touches

## New Marketing Channels (Being Built)

LEAD Advisors is investing in building six new marketing channels and capabilities that will expand reach, automate processes, and improve efficiency.

### Channel 1: Conference Intelligence & Networking

**Timeline:**
- **Q1:** Build and optimize initial process
- **Q2:** Ongoing optimization and expansion

**What It Is:**
- Strategic identification of key construction/industry conferences
- Intelligent routing of LEAD team to high-value events
- Pre-conference prospect research and scheduling
- Post-conference systematic follow-up and relationship building

**Goals:**
- Increase conference ROI
- Pre-schedule meetings before attending events
- Ensure systematic follow-up (not ad-hoc)
- Build relationships and brand awareness

**Implementation:**
- Create calendar of target conferences
- Develop pre-conference outreach sequence
- Prepare personalized talking points
- Systematic meeting scheduling
- Post-conference automation and CRM updates

**Expected Outcomes:**
- Higher-quality conference attendees identified
- Pre-scheduled meetings vs. booth interactions
- Better ROI on conference attendance
- Relationship continuity across multiple events

---

### Channel 2: Lead Vetting & Qualification

**Timeline:**
- **Q1:** Build process and criteria
- **Q2:** Optimize and scale

**What It Is:**
- AI-powered or manual vetting of leads before they reach sales
- Qualification based on 25-point scoring system
- Prioritization of P1/P2 leads
- Quick disqualification of poor-fit prospects

**Goals:**
- Ensure only qualified leads reach sales team
- Allow sales to focus on highest-probability opportunities
- Reduce sales cycle for obviously unqualified prospects
- Improve sales efficiency and close rate

**Implementation:**
- Establish vetting process and criteria
- Create vetting workflow (manual initially, AI-enabled eventually)
- Score all leads within 24 hours
- Route P1/P2 to sales immediately
- Route P3 to nurture
- Monitor <10 point leads for future opportunity

**Expected Outcomes:**
- Higher conversation-to-close rate
- More efficient sales pipeline
- Better data quality for sales team
- Faster time-to-value for prospects

The new marketing channels and optimization require significant technology development and infrastructure investment.

### Technology Component Details

#### Writing Agent (Q1)

**Purpose:** Auto-generate personalized outreach content (emails, LinkedIn messages, etc.)

**Functionality:**
- Analyze prospect data (company, role, industry, recent activity)
- Generate personalized email content based on trigger
- Create multiple subject line options
- Adapt tone based on prospect profile
- Save templates for reuse and A/B testing

**Integration:**
- Pull data from ZoomInfo/Apollo
- Input to Pipedrive for tracking
- Reduce manual writing time for outreach

**Expected Outcome:** 50% reduction in time spent writing outreach

---

#### Conference Agent (Q1)

**Purpose:** Identify target conferences and automate conference preparation

**Functionality:**
- Monitor industry conference calendar
- Score conferences for LEAD Advisors (which are most valuable)
- Identify key attendees expected at each conference
- Pre-populate prospect research for LEAD team
- Generate personalized outreach strategy for each event
- Track outcomes and ROI by conference

**Integration:**
- Pull data from ZoomInfo/Apollo
- Input to Pipedrive for opportunity tracking
- Connect with Writing Agent for pre-event outreach

**Expected Outcome:** 30% improvement in conference ROI; strategic focus on high-value events

---

#### Lead Enrichment (Q1)

**Purpose:** Automatically enrich lead data with relevant information

**Functionality:**
- Pull basic prospect list (from ZoomInfo/Apollo)
- Auto-enrich with company data (revenue, growth, news, etc.)
- Enrich with individual data (title, background, social profiles)
- Auto-detect trigger events (new hires, funding, etc.)
- Flag high-value signals automatically
- Create prospect summary for sales team

**Integration:**
- Pull data from ZoomInfo/Apollo/LinkedIn
- Store enriched data in Pipedrive
- Feed into Lead Scoring system

**Expected Outcome:** Richer prospect profiles; faster research for sales team; better-quality outreach

---

#### Lead Scoring (Q1-Q2)

**Purpose:** Automatically score leads based on 25-point system

**Functionality:**
- Auto-populate firmographic data (industry, revenue, size)
- Identify behavioral signals from company news and activity
- Assess cultural fit based on online presence and values signals
- Generate score for each lead
- Categorize into P1/P2/P3/Monitor
- Surface top opportunities for sales

**Integration:**
- Input enriched lead data
- Output to Pipedrive for routing
- Feed into Lead Vetting and routing workflow

**Expected Outcome:** Consistent, objective lead qualification; sales focuses on P1/P2 only

---

#### CRM Development (Q1-Q3)

**Purpose:** Enhanced Pipedrive configuration and customization

**Functionality:**
- Optimize Pipedrive for LEAD Advisors sales process
- Create custom fields for scoring, fit assessment, trigger type
- Build workflow automation for lead routing
- Create dashboards for pipeline visibility
- Integrate with other tools (ZoomInfo, Apollo, etc.)
- Build reporting and analytics

**Integration:**
- Central hub for all marketing and sales data
- Integration point for agents and automation
- Source of truth for pipeline and metrics

**Expected Outcome:** Single source of truth for sales pipeline; better visibility and reporting

---

#### System Integration (Q2-Q3)

**Purpose:** Connect all tools and platforms into cohesive marketing-to-sales system

**Components:**
- ZoomInfo → Lead Enrichment → Lead Scoring → Pipedrive
- Apollo → Lead Enrichment → Lead Scoring → Pipedrive
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator → Lead Research → Pipedrive
- Writing Agent → Generated content → Email/LinkedIn
- Conference Agent → Conference strategy → Pipedrive
- HubSpot (if used) → Integrated with Pipedrive
- All dashboards connected for unified analytics

**Expected Outcome:** Seamless data flow; reduced manual work; better insights

---

#### Automation (Q3-Q4)

**Purpose:** Automate routine, repeatable marketing and sales tasks

**Automation Examples:**
- Automatic lead scoring on new prospects
- Automatic P1/P2 routing to sales
- Automatic P3/Monitor added to nurture sequences
- Automatic email sequences for follow-up
- Automatic LinkedIn outreach based on engagement
- Automatic event reminders and scheduling
- Automatic report generation and distribution

**Expected Outcome:** 60%+ reduction in manual sales administration; focus on high-value activities

## Marketing Success Metrics

### Lead Volume Metrics
- **New leads identified per month** – Target: 40-50 leads/month in steady state
- **P1 leads per month** – Target: 8-10 P1 leads/month
- **P2 leads per month** – Target: 12-15 P2 leads/month
- **P3 leads per month** – Target: 10-15 P3 leads/month

### Lead Quality Metrics
- **Outreach response rate** – Target: 15-20%
- **Discovery call rate** – Target: 30-40% of responses
- **Proposal rate** – Target: 50-60% of discovery calls
- **Close rate** – Target: 50-60% of proposals

### Pipeline Metrics
- **Sales pipeline value** – Target: $500K-$750K in active pipeline
- **Monthly closed revenue** – Target: $50K-$75K/month average
- **Sales cycle length** – Target: 3-6 months for P1, 6-12 months for P2

### Efficiency Metrics
- **Cost per lead** – Track and optimize
- **Cost per closed deal** – Target: <20% of deal value
- **Lead to close conversion rate** – Target: 5-10% overall

### Channel Metrics (by source)
- **Conference ROI** – Revenue from conference leads / conference costs
- **LinkedIn effectiveness** – Conversion rate of LinkedIn-sourced leads
- **Job posting** – Deals closed from job posting trigger
- **Leadership change** – Deals closed from leadership change trigger
- **Four-Fires community** – Deals closed from referral source
