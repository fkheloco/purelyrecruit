# Opportunity Qualification Scoring System

> **Purpose:** A standardized 100-point scoring system for objectively evaluating every prospect. Ensures consistent qualification across all five ICPs and removes bias from prioritization decisions.

---

## Scoring Overview

| Category | Max Points | Weight |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| Firmographic Fit | 40 | 40% |
| Behavioral Signals | 35 | 35% |
| Cultural Fit | 25 | 25% |
| **Total** | **100** | **100%** |

---

## Category 1: Firmographic Fit (40 Points)

### Revenue Range (15 points)
| Range | Points |
|-------|--------|
| $30M-$200M (sweet spot) | 15 |
| $10M-$30M | 12 |
| $200M-$500M | 10 |
| $5M-$10M | 7 |
| <$5M or >$500M | 3 |

### Industry Alignment (10 points)
| Alignment | Points |
|-----------|--------|
| Target ICP industry (Construction, Staffing, SaaS, Entertainment, Biotech) | 10 |
| Adjacent industry with clear service fit | 7 |
| General professional services | 4 |
| No clear industry fit | 1 |

### Company Size (8 points)
| Employee Count | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| 50-300 (ideal) | 8 |
| 25-50 | 6 |
| 300-500 | 5 |
| <25 or >500 | 2 |

### Geographic Fit (7 points)
| Location | Points |
|----------|--------|
| SoCal (LA, OC, SD counties) | 7 |
| California (other) | 5 |
| West Coast / Mountain time zone | 4 |
| Other US | 3 |
| International | 1 |

---

## Category 2: Behavioral Signals (35 Points)

### Trigger Events (15 points)
| Signal | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Recent funding round (within 90 days) | 15 |
| Major contract win/loss (within 60 days) | 13 |
| New senior leader hired (within 90 days) | 12 |
| Expansion announcement | 10 |
| Job postings matching our services | 8 |
| Industry event attendance | 5 |
| No recent trigger events | 2 |

### Service Need Indicators (12 points)
| Signal | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Actively seeking the service we offer (RFP, job post for role we'd fill) | 12 |
| Stated pain point we can solve (interview, LinkedIn post, press) | 10 |
| Implied need (growing fast, hiring, new market) | 7 |
| General "might benefit" assessment | 4 |
| No clear need signal | 1 |

### Decision-Maker Access (8 points)
| Access Level | Points |
|-------------|--------|
| Direct warm intro to decision-maker | 8 |
| Warm intro to influencer | 6 |
| LinkedIn connection (2nd degree) | 4 |
| Cold outreach only | 2 |

---

## Category 3: Cultural Fit (25 Points)

### Partnership Mentality (10 points)
| Signal | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Uses partnership language, collaborative culture | 10 |
| Open to external partners, history of agency use | 7 |
| Mixed signals | 4 |
| Strongly DIY / in-house only culture | 1 |

### Quality Focus (8 points)
| Signal | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Values quality, willing to invest for results | 8 |
| Balanced quality/cost consideration | 5 |
| Primarily cost-driven decisions | 2 |

### AI Openness (7 points)
| Signal | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Actively investing in AI, AI-positive leadership | 7 |
| Curious about AI, early experiments | 5 |
| Neutral / undecided on AI | 3 |
| AI-skeptical or resistant | 1 |

---

## Priority Tiers

| Tier | Score Range | Action |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Priority 1** | 80-100 | Immediate outreach within 48 hours |
| **Priority 2** | 60-79 | Active monitoring, engage on next trigger event |
| **Priority 3** | 40-59 | Passive monitoring, quarterly check-in |
| **Not a Fit** | Below 40 | Archive, revisit in 6 months |

---
