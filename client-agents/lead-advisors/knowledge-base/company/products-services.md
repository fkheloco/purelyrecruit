# LEAD Advisors Products & Services

## Service Overview

LEAD Advisors offers a comprehensive suite of leadership development and organizational consulting services, all anchored in the Four-Fires Philosophy. Each service is designed to develop exceptional organizations by building exceptional cultures, teams, leaders, and individuals.

---

## 1. Management Training

**Program Duration:** 12 months

**Overview:**
The flagship service providing systematic, customized leadership development for manager-level and emerging leader populations.

**Program Structure:**
- Customized curriculum tailored to client's specific culture, challenges, and development needs
- 12-month engagement with regular coaching, training, and assessment activities
- Combination of individual coaching, group training sessions, and peer learning
- Integration of the Four-Fires Philosophy throughout the curriculum

**Typical Investment:** ~$50,000 per engagement

**Key Features:**
- Holistic leadership development (Purpose, People, Physical, Professional)
- Sustainable behavior change and culture integration
- Measurable progress through assessments and feedback
- Long-term accountability and reinforcement
- Custom curriculum designed to address specific organizational challenges

**Ideal For:**
- Companies with multiple managers needing coordinated development
- Organizations transitioning or scaling
- Companies promoting operational leaders into management roles
- Teams struggling with communication or culture issues

**Four-Fires Philosophy Integration:**
Each participant learns to develop themselves and lead others across all four fires:
- Understanding personal and organizational purpose
- Building healthy relationships and teams
- Recognizing importance of health and wellness for leadership effectiveness
- Developing professional skills and business acumen

## Four-Fires Philosophy in Every Service

Regardless of which service is selected, the Four-Fires Philosophy ensures that development is:
- **Holistic** – Addressing the whole person, not just professional skills
- **Sustainable** – Creating lasting change vs. temporary behavior modification
- **Integrated** – Connecting personal values and wellness with organizational success
- **Measurable** – Clear outcomes across all four dimensions

The result: Organizations that are truly exceptional, built on foundations of exceptional cultures, teams, leaders, and individuals.
