# Email Signature Guide

## Signature Variations

Use the appropriate signature based on your audience and relationship level.

---

### Full Signature (Formal - New Contacts, Executives)

```
--
Farid Kheloco
CEO / Co-Founder
Purely Works

Helping You Build Smarter with AI
www.purelyworks.com
```

**When to use:**
- First-touch emails to new prospects
- Communication with C-level executives
- Legal or formal business communications
- Partnership proposals
- External announcements

---

### Semi-Formal (Established Business Relationships)

```
All the best,

Farid
```

**When to use:**
- Ongoing client relationships
- Established partner communications
- Follow-up emails in active threads
- Business contacts you've met with before

---

### Casual Internal (Team Communications)

```
-Farid
```

**When to use:**
- Internal team messages
- Slack communications (if using email-to-Slack)
- Quick updates to colleagues
- Informal check-ins

---

## HTML Signature

For use in email clients that support HTML signatures, see `signature.html` in this folder.

**Installation:**
1. Open your email client settings
2. Navigate to signature settings
3. Copy/paste the HTML from signature.html
4. Test by sending yourself an email

**Supported clients:**
- Gmail (web and desktop)
- Outlook (web and desktop)
- Apple Mail
- Superhuman
- Most modern email clients
