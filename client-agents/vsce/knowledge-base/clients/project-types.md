## Project Delivery Phases

### Planning Phase
**Duration:** 6-24 months
**VSCE Services:** Project management, feasibility analysis, community engagement support
**Key Deliverables:** Master plan, feasibility study, environmental documentation

### Design Phase
**Duration:** 12-36 months
**VSCE Services:** Design oversight, stakeholder coordination, permitting support
**Key Deliverables:** Final design documents, permits, bid-ready packages

### Procurement Phase
**Duration:** 2-6 months
**VSCE Services:** RFP development, evaluation, contractor selection support
**Key Deliverables:** RFP document, evaluation results, award recommendation

### Construction Phase
**Duration:** 6 months - 3+ years
**VSCE Services:** Construction management, resident engineer, quality oversight
**Key Deliverables:** Progress reports, inspection documentation, as-built records

### Post-Construction
**Duration:** 3-6 months
**VSCE Services:** Project closeout, lessons learned, final documentation
**Key Deliverables:** As-built documentation, final reports, knowledge transfer

## Service Integration Across Projects

### Typical Project Lifecycle with VSCE

**Initial Engagement (Year 1)**
- Single project assignment (often construction management or analysis)
- Demonstrates capability and reliability
- Builds relationship with project team

**Relationship Development (Year 2-3)**
- Additional projects or expanded scope
- Movement toward on-call relationship
- Integration into agency planning and decision-making

**Strategic Partnership (Year 5+)**
- Multiple concurrent projects
- On-call retainer for program management and advisory
- Involvement at leadership/board level
- Integrated across multiple service lines

### Example: Multi-Service Engagement Pattern

1. **Year 1:** Construction management on Transit Center ($300K project)
2. **Year 2:** Capital program analysis for 5-year CIP ($150K)
3. **Year 2-3:** Program manager on-site for capital program ($500K+ annually)
4. **Year 3+:** On-call retainer for program management + project-specific services

## Project Selection Criteria

### Ideal Project Characteristics
- ✓ Public-sector transportation focus
- ✓ $50K+ project value
- ✓ Aligns with VSCE service offerings
- ✓ Geographic fit (CA or PacNW)
- ✓ Clear budget and decision authority
- ✓ Professional project management
- ✓ Opportunity for relationship development

### Projects to Avoid
- ✗ Private sector (outside market focus)
- ✗ Very small (<$25K, not economic)
- ✗ Outside service offerings (e.g., heavy engineering design)
- ✗ Geographic mismatch
- ✗ Unclear budget or decision authority
- ✗ Significant capital investment from VSCE
- ✗ High execution risk
