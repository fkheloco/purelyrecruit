# VSCE, Inc. - Products & Services Catalog

## Service Overview

VSCE provides comprehensive consulting and management services to public-sector transportation agencies and government entities. Services are organized around core competencies and typically involve staff augmentation, consulting engagement, and program management models.

### Company Financial Profile
- **Estimated Annual Revenue:** $50M+ (based on team size, utilization, and billable rates)
- **Profitability:** High-margin consulting model
- **Growth Rate:** 15-20% annual expansion (SoCal and PacNW initiatives)
- **Financial Model:** Primarily time-and-materials with strategic fixed-fee projects

### Service Distribution by Percentage
- **Construction Management:** 40% of service revenue
- **Program Management:** 30% of service revenue
- **Public Relations/Outreach:** 15% of service revenue
- **Quality Assurance:** 8% of service revenue
- **Labor Compliance:** 5% of service revenue
- **Specialized Engineering:** 2% of service revenue

## Service Pricing Models

### Time-and-Materials
- Hourly billing for professional services
- Typical rates: $150-$300/hour depending on expertise
- Invoiced monthly with timesheets
- Flexible scope and duration

### Fixed-Fee Projects
- Project-based pricing
- Defined scope and deliverables
- Typically for studies, analyses, or short-term engagements
- More predictable for client budget planning

### Retainer Agreements
- Monthly fee for committed availability
- Often combined with time-and-materials overages
- Typically $5,000-$15,000/month for part-time engagement
- Provides priority access and response time

### Staff Augmentation
- Typically employee-of-record or invoice-pass-through
- Based on hourly rates or monthly salary
- Can be on VSCE payroll or direct to client
- Flexibility in duration and scope
