## Color Usage Guidelines

**Primary Palette:**
- Metallic Gold + Chambray should dominate all branded materials
- Use 60% Chambray, 30% Metallic Gold, 10% accents as a general rule

**Accent Colors:**
- Use sparingly for highlights, callouts, and visual interest
- Never use more than 2-3 accent colors in a single piece
- Match accent to message tone:
  - Fire Bush / Fuzzy Wuzzy: Warmth, approachability
  - Soft Purple: Innovation, creativity
  - Pacific Blue: Trust, technology
  - Keppel: Growth, success

**Accessibility:**
- Always test text contrast ratios (WCAG AA minimum: 4.5:1)
- Use Chambray for body text on light backgrounds
- Use white text on Metallic Gold or Chambray backgrounds
