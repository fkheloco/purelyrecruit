## Key Information at a Glance

### The Product
- **Name:** Cathedral Center (Project Cathedral, Project Cathy)
- **What It Does:** AI-powered assessment platform for financial advisor M&A and recruiting decisions
- **Launch Date:** April 1, 2026
- **Value Proposition:** "Transform your recruiting and M&A process by making assessments feel like valuable consulting rather than sales"

### The Problem
- ~70% of advisor M&A partnerships fail within 5 years
- Most decisions based on financial metrics alone
- Inadequate cultural fit and compatibility assessment

### The Solution
- Three-tier assessment (public data, questionnaire, interviews)
- DNA scoring across five dimensions (culture, values, behavior, needs, desires)
- AI-augmented reporting and integration planning
- Consultative engagement model

## Key Compliance & Important Notes

### Financial Services Sensitivity
- All assessments are confidential internal planning tools
- Advisor data and DNA scores are never shared without explicit consent
- Frame assessments as consulting insights, not judgmental evaluations
- Respect advisor privacy and professional standing

### LPL Financial Awareness
- SageCrest is LPL-affiliated
- Acknowledge affiliation in formal contexts
- Never position Cathedral Center as an LPL product
- It's a SageCrest innovation for the broader advisory market

### "Consulting Not Sales" Principle
- All communications should feel like valuable expert guidance
- Lead with insight, not product features
- Position assessment as adding value regardless of partnership outcome
- Build trust through educational engagement
