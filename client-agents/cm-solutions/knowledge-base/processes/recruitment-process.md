# CM Solutions - Recruitment & Staff Augmentation Process

## Overview

This document outlines CM Solutions' recruitment and staff augmentation processes, including workflow, responsibilities, and AI automation initiatives with Purely Works' Mammoth Recruiting Solution.

## RECRUITMENT STRATEGY & PHILOSOPHY

### Core Values

**Diversity & Inclusion:**
- Target diverse candidate pools
- Women-friendly recruiting messaging
- Inclusive language (non-gendered)
- Active recruitment from underrepresented groups
- Promote 40%+ women workforce

**Quality & Fit:**
- Prioritize cultural fit and values alignment
- Seek experienced, credentialed professionals
- Look for PSP, PE, and relevant certifications
- Assess problem-solving and collaboration skills
- Verify past performance and references

**Growth & Development:**
- Career advancement opportunities
- Professional development support
- Certification and credentialing support
- Mentorship and leadership development
- Long-term career path clarity

### Target Candidate Profiles

**Project Schedulers / PSP Candidates**
- PSP certified or PSP-eligible
- 5+ years construction scheduling experience
- Primavera P6 expertise
- Complex project experience
- Problem-solving orientation

**Cost Controllers / Budget Analysts**
- 3+ years cost control experience
- Earned value management (EVM) knowledge
- Construction budgeting and forecasting
- Data analysis and reporting skills
- Risk management understanding

**Project Managers / Project Controls Managers**
- 5+ years project management experience
- PMP or equivalent credential
- Construction industry background
- Multi-disciplinary team leadership
- Client relationship skills

**Claims & Scheduling Specialists**
- Delay analysis and expert testimony experience
- Advanced scheduling knowledge
- Claims development expertise
- Litigation support capability
- CMS-relevant experience

## RECRUITMENT WORKFLOW

### Phase 1: WORKFORCE PLANNING & NEED IDENTIFICATION

**Trigger:** Resource gap, project requirement, or strategic growth plan

**Key Activities:**

1. **Staffing Need Definition**
   - Position type and level
   - Required skills and credentials
   - Experience requirements
   - Timeline for hiring
   - Budget allocation

2. **Role Description Development**
   - Position title and description
   - Primary responsibilities
   - Key competencies
   - Desired experience level
   - Compensation range

3. **Hiring Manager Briefing**
   - Interview process planning
   - Selection criteria
   - Timeline and timeline expectations
   - Budget approval
   - Offer authorization limits

**Responsible Parties:**
- Joe Schlegel (VP Talent Acquisition) — overall coordination
- Hiring manager — needs definition
- HR (Margrit Lehmann) — HR policy and process
- Finance — budget allocation

**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks

**Output:** Job description and hiring plan

---

### Phase 2: JOB DESCRIPTION DEVELOPMENT

**Key Activities:**

1. **Position Overview**
   - Title and reporting structure
   - Department and team context
   - Role importance and impact
   - Career development path

2. **Responsibilities Definition**
   - Primary responsibilities (bulleted)
   - Key projects and activities
   - Client interaction expectations
   - Travel and location requirements
   - Schedule expectations

3. **Qualifications & Experience**
   - Required experience (years, type)
   - Desired experience (nice-to-have)
   - Education requirements
   - Certifications (PSP, PE, PMP, etc.)
   - Skills (technical and soft)

4. **Compensation & Benefits**
   - Salary range or pay level
   - Benefits overview
   - Professional development support
   - Certification support
   - Work arrangement (remote, office, hybrid)

5. **Diversity & Inclusion Language**
   - Inclusive, non-gendered language
   - Equal opportunity statement
   - Commitment to diverse hiring
   - Women-friendly positioning
   - Accessibility accommodations

**Responsible Parties:**
- Hiring manager — content development
- Joe Schlegel — recruiting strategy and positioning
- HR — policy compliance and standard language
- Legal (if applicable) — compliance review

**Timeline:** 1 week typical

**Output:** Final job description ready for posting

---

### Phase 3: RECRUITING OUTREACH

**Key Activities:**

1. **Job Posting & Distribution**
   - Internal posting (if promoting from within)
   - External job boards (LinkedIn, Indeed, etc.)
   - Professional associations (AACE, others)
   - Industry referral networks
   - University career services (if entry-level)

2. **Direct Outreach & Sourcing**
   - Headhunter/recruiter engagement (if specialized role)
   - Networking outreach (industry contacts)
   - Internal referral program promotion
   - Competitor target outreach (if appropriate)

3. **Candidate Pipeline Building**
   - Networking events and conferences
   - University recruiting programs
   - Professional association connections
   - Alumni networks
   - Staff referral incentives

4. **Applicant Tracking & Management**
   - Collect resumes and applications
   - Initial screening for fit
   - Track progress through process
   - CRM integration (Salesforce)

**Responsible Parties:**
- Joe Schlegel — overall sourcing strategy
- Recruiters — outreach and sourcing
- HR — job postings and applicant tracking
- Team members — referral program

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks (ongoing)

**Output:** Candidate pipeline and applications

---

### Phase 4: SCREENING & SELECTION

**Key Activities:**

1. **Resume Screening**
   - Initial fit assessment
   - Experience verification
   - Credential checking
   - Red flag identification
   - Ranking/prioritization

2. **Phone / Initial Interviews**
   - Experience and background verification
   - Motivation and interest assessment
   - Cultural fit questions
   - Logistics and timeline questions
   - Schedule for next steps

3. **Technical Interviews**
   - Relevant technical skills assessment
   - Problem-solving ability
   - Tool proficiency (P6, etc.)
   - Experience depth verification
   - Scenario-based questions

4. **In-Person / Panel Interviews**
   - Multiple stakeholder perspectives
   - Cultural fit assessment
   - Leadership evaluation (if applicable)
   - Collaboration and teamwork
   - Long-term potential assessment

5. **Reference Checks**
   - Contact past employers/managers
   - Verify experience and accomplishments
   - Assess performance and reliability
   - Cultural fit feedback
   - Red flag identification

**Interview Panel Composition:**
- Hiring manager (primary)
- Peer or team member
- HR representative
- Senior leader (for senior positions)

**Selection Criteria:**
- Required skills and experience match
- Cultural fit and values alignment
- Growth potential and ambition
- Communication and collaboration
- Problem-solving and initiative
- Certification status (PSP, PE, etc.)

**Responsible Parties:**
- Hiring manager — primary interview and selection
- Joe Schlegel — process coordination and candidate fit
- HR — process management and reference checks
- Team members — technical and peer assessment

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks typical

**Output:** Offer-ready candidate(s) selected

---

### Phase 5: OFFER & NEGOTIATION

**Key Activities:**

1. **Offer Development**
   - Base salary determination
   - Benefits package
   - Sign-on bonus (if applicable)
   - Performance metrics/expectations
   - Start date

2. **Offer Presentation**
   - Offer meeting with candidate
   - Compensation package review
   - Benefits explanation
   - Career path discussion
   - Timeline for decision

3. **Negotiation (If Applicable)**
   - Salary negotiation
   - Benefits adjustment
   - Start date flexibility
   - Work arrangement negotiation
   - Professional development support

4. **Offer Acceptance & Documentation**
   - Offer letter signed
   - Background check authorization
   - Drug screening (if applicable)
   - I-9 and employment verification
   - Confidentiality/non-compete agreements (if applicable)

5. **Onboarding Planning**
   - Start date confirmation
   - Equipment and access ordering
   - First-day logistics
   - Onboarding schedule development
   - Manager preparation

**Responsible Parties:**
- Joe Schlegel — offer strategy and negotiation
- HR — offer documentation and background checks
- Hiring manager — final approval and negotiations
- Finance — compensation approval

**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks from offer to acceptance

**Output:** Accepted offer and start date confirmed

### Phase 6: ONBOARDING & INTEGRATION

**Key Activities:**

1. **Pre-Arrival Preparation**
   - Equipment ordering (laptop, phone, etc.)
   - Workstation setup
   - Access/security provisioning
   - Email and system accounts created
   - Welcome packet preparation

2. **First Day**
   - Welcome and orientation
   - HR paperwork completion (benefits, payroll, etc.)
   - Safety and compliance training
   - IT setup and access verification
   - Team introductions

3. **First Week**
   - Full orientation program
   - Department and company overview
   - Team introductions
   - Projects and client overview
   - Learning plan development

4. **First 30 Days**
   - Project assignment and ramp-up
   - Mentorship/buddy assignment
   - Training and tool proficiency
   - Team integration
   - Manager check-in and feedback

5. **First 90 Days**
   - Project performance assessment
   - Skills development progress
   - Cultural fit verification
   - Goal setting for first year
   - Probationary review

**Responsible Parties:**
- HR — onboarding coordination
- Hiring manager — project assignment and oversight
- Team lead — day-to-day integration
- Mentor/buddy — support and guidance

**Timeline:** 90 days to full productivity

**Output:** Integrated team member contributing effectively

## STAFF AUGMENTATION / TEMPORARY STAFFING

### Staff Augmentation Model

**When Used:**
- Project peaks requiring temporary staff
- Specialized expertise for specific duration
- Flexibility without permanent hire
- Quick fill of unexpected gaps

### Staffing Categories

**Project Controls Specialists**
- Senior Schedulers (PSP-certified)
- Cost Controllers/Budget Analysts
- Risk Specialists
- Scheduling Analysts

**Administrative & Support**
- Document Control Specialists
- Administrative Coordinators
- Data Analysis & Reporting

**Project Management**
- Assistant Project Managers
- Proposal Coordinators
- Administrative Project Managers

### Staffing Process

1. **Need Definition**
   - Duration (weeks, months, years)
   - Required skills and experience
   - Rate and budget
   - Start date

2. **Resource Identification**
   - Internal staff availability (first priority)
   - Contractor/temp agency (if needed)
   - Industry network outreach
   - Screened candidate pool

3. **Placement & Integration**
   - Brief assignment and expectations
   - Team introduction
   - Work arrangement confirmation
   - Reporting structure clarity

4. **Ongoing Management**
   - Performance check-ins
   - Quality assurance
   - Scope management
   - Rate and billing verification

5. **Transition/Closeout**
   - Knowledge transfer documentation
   - Equipment and access removal
   - Final invoice and closeout
   - Feedback on contractor

**Responsible Parties:**
- Joe Schlegel — sourcing and recruitment
- Hiring manager — assignment and management
- HR — compliance and documentation
