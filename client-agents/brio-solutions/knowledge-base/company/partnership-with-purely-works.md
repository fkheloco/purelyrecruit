# Brio Solutions - Partnership with Purely Works

## Overview

Brio Solutions works with Purely Works for AI solutions, recruiting support, and strategic advisory services.

**Partnership Type:** AI Solutions Provider, Recruiting Partner, Strategic Advisor
**Engagement Start:** Late 2025
**Key Services:** AI agent development, talent acquisition, business strategy consulting

---

## Services Provided

### 1. AI Solutions Development

Purely Works is building a suite of 6 AI agents to support Brio's teaming partnership development and operations:

**AI Agents:**
1. **RFP Response Agent** - Accelerates proposal development (50% time reduction)
2. **ICP & Lead Research Agent** - Partner identification and intelligence (60% time reduction)
3. **Candidate Screening Agent** - Talent pipeline evaluation (70% time reduction)
4. **Sales Intelligence Agent** - Competitive analysis and outreach strategy (40% time reduction)
5. **Conference Research Agent** - Event intelligence and networking prep (50% time reduction)
6. **Market Research Agent** - Strategic market analysis (60% time reduction)

**Technology Stack:**
* ChatGPT Plus (GPT-4) - Primary AI platform for all agents
* HubSpot Starter - CRM and pipeline management
* Integration with existing tools (Google Workspace, Microsoft Copilot)

**Expected Impact:**
* 10-20 hours/week time savings across operations
* Faster RFP response cycles
* Better partner targeting and research
* Streamlined recruiting pipeline

### 2. Recruiting Services

Purely Works provides talent acquisition support for Brio's staff augmentation business:

**Recruiting Focus:**
* Construction managers (aviation/airport experience preferred)
* Project managers (PMP certified, infrastructure background)
* Resident engineers
* Project control specialists
* PMIS analysts and implementation managers

**Recruiting Process:**
* Weekly candidate sourcing and screening
* Airtable-based candidate management
* Detailed recruiter notes and assessments
* Client-ready candidate presentations

**Team:**
* Armeen Haroon - Lead Recruiter
* Regular sync meetings (Wednesdays 10:30 AM PST)

### 3. Strategic Advisory

Purely Works provides business strategy consulting and operational guidance:

**Advisory Areas:**
* Partner development strategy
* Go-to-market approach
* Proposal and RFP response optimization
* Sales process improvement
* Technology and tool selection
* Operational efficiency

**Key Contacts:**
* Farid Kheloco - Account Manager & Strategic Advisor
* David Hirsch - Client Representative (coordinates across services)
* Hammad Younas - Development Lead (AI agents, tools)
