# Purely Adventure — Company Overview

## About

Purely Adventure is an AI-powered adventure media platform that delivers personalized, curated content across overlanding, adventure boating (power and sail), and adventure travel.

Each user builds their own concept of adventure — choosing their interests, filtering by category, and receiving daily content tailored to what they love. No toxic comments, no noise, just purely adventure.

## Mission

Your personalized home for everything adventure — curated daily, built around what you love.

## Vision

**Year 1:** Revenue-generating platform with a solid content library, growing traffic, and tasteful monetization through ads and affiliate links.

**Year 3:** A thriving community where adventurers geek out on their passions, contribute stories, share reviews, and connect — with a premium ad-free tier supporting sustainable growth.

## Core Categories

* **Land** — Overlanding, off-road travel, expedition vehicles, trails, and gear
* **Sea** — Adventure boating, sailing, powerboats, coastal exploration, and marine gear
* **Air** — Aviation adventures, paragliding, skydiving, drone exploration
* **Travel** — Adventure destinations, trip planning, itineraries, budget and logistics

## What Makes Us Different

* **Personalized experience** — Users curate their own adventure feed based on interests
* **AI-curated daily content** — RSS feeds scanned and compiled into unique daily stories per category
* **No noise** — Moderated community, no toxic comments, no Facebook-style drama
* **Family-friendly filtering** — Content flagged for family-friendly or general audiences
* **Practical and authentic** — Real information, honest reviews, no sponsored fluff

## Business Model

* AI-powered content aggregation and creation
* Monetized through tasteful display ads and affiliate links (free tier)
* Future premium tier: pay to remove ads ("support us and shut down the noise")
* Sponsor message sections within daily digests
* Affiliate partnerships with gear brands, booking platforms, and adventure companies
