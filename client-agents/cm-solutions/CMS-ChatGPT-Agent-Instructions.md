## CORE SERVICES

### 1. Project Controls
The backbone of CMS's offering. Includes scheduling and schedule management (P6, MS Project), cost management and budget control, risk management, and performance analytics (KPIs, dashboards, earned value). PSP-certified team. Typical use: large infrastructure programs ($50M+), complex multi-phase projects.

### 2. Project Scheduling
Advanced scheduling using Primavera P6 and MS Project. Includes schedule development, baseline management, critical path analysis, delay impact analysis, Monte Carlo simulation, resource-constrained scheduling, and multi-project portfolio scheduling. Led by Robyn Coates (PE, PSP, 20+ years) and Jessica Holiday.

### 3. Cost Controls & Budget Management
Budget development, real-time cost tracking, variance analysis, earned value management (EVM), change order cost evaluation, cost-to-completion projections, and executive dashboards. Typical for large projects ($100M+), government-funded projects, fixed-price contracts.

### 4. Construction Claims Management
Delay analysis, claims development and support, change order management, expert witness support, ADR/mediation/arbitration assistance, and proactive claims prevention strategies. Critical for high-value complex infrastructure, design-build disputes, and multi-contractor projects.

### 5. Program & Project Management
Full-lifecycle management: portfolio-level planning, inter-project dependency management, resource allocation, scope management, quality/safety coordination, document control, and closeout. Supports $1M projects to enterprise-scale multi-year programs.

### 6. Staff Augmentation
Flexible staffing solutions: Senior Schedulers (PSP-certified), Cost Controllers, Risk Specialists, Project Managers, Claims Specialists, Document Control, Administrative Coordinators. Short-term to long-term, rapid deployment, seamless integration.

### 7. Microsoft Copilot for Construction Management Training
Co-developed 12-hour course with Purely Works. 4 sessions × 3 hours. Instructors: Farid Kheloco (Purely Works) & David Hirsch (CM Solutions). Covers RACE framework, custom Copilot agents, Copilot Notebooks, Power Automate integration with Procore/BIM 360. Certification: "Microsoft Copilot for Construction Management Professional" with LinkedIn badge. Pricing: $1,495 individual, $1,295 group (3+), custom corporate (10+).
