## Initial Brief

**Business Concept:** AI-powered adventure media platform — personalized, curated daily content across overlanding, adventure boating, and adventure travel. Monetized through ads and affiliate links with a future premium ad-free tier.

**Current Stage:** Early startup planning — discovery complete, knowledge base built.

---

## Discovery Answers

### BATCH 1: Core Concept & Mission

**1. What type of adventures?**
Overlanding, Adventure Travel, and Adventure Boating (Power and Sail). Organized as Land, Sea, Air, and Travel categories.

**2. Main problem solved?**
Creating a personalized space where people can follow their adventure passions. Each user builds their own concept of adventure — with news, stories, trips, reviews, and user-generated moderated content. A positive community free from toxic social media noise. "Purely adventure."

**3. What makes it different?**
Personalized experience where each user curates their feed. AI-curated daily content. No toxic comments or noise. Family-friendly filtering. Practical, authentic, no sponsored fluff. It's a haven for people to learn and geek out on whatever they consider adventure.

**4. Ideal visitor/reader?**
Mix of beginners (priority), families, and experienced adventurers. People passionate about overlanding, boating, and travel who want a trusted, positive space.

**5. Mission statement?**
"Your personalized home for everything adventure — curated daily, built around what you love."

**6. 1-year and 3-year vision?**
Year 1: Revenue-generating platform with solid content library and growing traffic.
Year 3: Thriving community with user-generated content, premium tier, and sustainable income.

---

### BATCH 2: Content Strategy

**7. Content types?**
Daily news digests per category, destination guides, gear reviews, trip itineraries, how-to guides, product updates, adventure stories, comparison guides.

**8. AI-generated or human?**
AI-first. AI scans RSS feeds daily and compiles unique stories. Founder oversees quality. Future user-generated content (moderated).

**9. Content formats?**
Articles, daily email digests, social media posts, short-form video (Shorts/Reels), interactive filtering/personalization on site.

**10. Publishing frequency?**
Daily digests per category. Weekly in-depth guides. Monthly seasonal roundups.

**11. Geographic focus?**
Global, starting US-centric. Expanding based on audience demand.

**12. Adventure types?**
Overlanding, boating (power and sail), adventure travel. Organized as Land, Sea, Air, Travel.

**13. Experience level?**
All levels: beginners first, then families, then experienced adventurers.

---

### BATCH 3: Monetization Strategy

**14. Ad types?**
Tasteful display ads — Google AdSense initially, upgrading to premium networks (Mediavine, AdThrive) as traffic grows. Never intrusive.

**15. Affiliate programs?**
Amazon Associates, REI, West Marine, booking platforms, gear brand programs. Natural placement in reviews and product spotlights.

**16. Other revenue?**
Premium ad-free tier ("support us and shut down the noise"). Sponsor message sections in daily digests.

**17. Revenue model?**
CPM and CPC. Traffic targets: 10K sessions at month 6, 50K at month 12, 100K+ at month 18.

**18. Priority affiliates?**
Outdoor gear (REI, Amazon), marine gear (West Marine), booking platforms, adventure gear brands.

**19. Direct partnerships?**
Future — sponsor messages in digests, brand partnerships as audience grows.

---

### BATCH 4: AI & Technology

**20. AI usage?**
RSS feed scanning, content aggregation, daily digest generation, social media post creation, affiliate link insertion, SEO optimization, publishing automation.

**21. AI tools?**
Claude API (primary), with workflow automation via Zapier/Make.com. Video generation via Synthesia/Descript.

**22. AI vs. human?**
AI handles: content generation, publishing, social distribution, SEO, scheduling. Human handles: quality review (30 min/day), strategy, editorial direction, sponsor relationships.

**23. Website platform?**
Open to custom-built or WordPress. Priority: agentic-driven system where .MD files are input and the agent handles everything else.

**24. Special features?**
Personalized feed filtering by interest, family-friendly content flagging, user-generated content (moderated), daily email digests.

---

### BATCH 5: Audience & Marketing

**25. Primary audience?**
Beginners first (25-45, newly interested), then families (30-50), then experienced adventurers (30-60). All income levels.

**26. Pain points?**
Too much scattered info online, hard to find trustworthy recommendations, intimidated by expert communities, tired of toxic social media noise, adventure seems inaccessible.

**27. Current sources?**
Overland Journal, Expedition Portal, Cruising World, Sail Magazine, Reddit communities, YouTube, Facebook groups (frustrated by noise), Instagram.

**28. Traffic strategy?**
All channels: SEO, social media, email newsletter, potentially paid ads. AI-first distribution.

**29. Social platforms?**
Tier 1: Reddit, YouTube (Shorts + Community), Instagram (Reels + Feed), Email Newsletter.
Tier 2: Bluesky (monitor).
Not recommended: TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn, Pinterest.

**30. Influencer partnerships?**
Future consideration as audience grows. Not a priority for Year 1.

### BATCH 8: Competition & Differentiation

**43. Main competitors?**
Overland Journal / Expedition Portal (overlanding), Cruising World / Sail Magazine (boating/sailing).

**44. What they do well?**
Deep niche content, established authority, loyal audiences.

**45. Gaps to fill?**
No personalization. No cross-category adventure hub. No AI-curated daily digests. Most are single-niche. None offer the "build your own adventure feed" concept. Community moderation is weak.

**46. Unique advantage?**
AI-powered personalization, cross-category coverage (land/sea/air/travel), daily curated digests, positive moderated community, solo-operator efficiency through AI.
