# Brio Solutions - Company Overview

**Website:** gobriosolutions.com

**Company Size:** Fewer than 25 employees

## Mission & Approach

Brio Solutions provides innovative software solutions and construction management services to help businesses streamline their operations and improve efficiency. The company believes in the use of latest technology and innovative approaches to Program and Construction Management.

Their solutions are designed and customized for project needs and are scalable to all sizes of projects. Brio Solutions partners with clients throughout projects, focusing on developing effective strategies and scalable solutions tailored to specific project requirements.

## Business Model: Teaming & Subcontractor Partnerships

Brio Solutions operates as a specialized PMIS implementation and project controls subcontractor for prime contractors bidding on major government infrastructure contracts.

**Core Business Model:**
* **Partnership Approach:** Brio teams with prime contractors (engineering firms, PMCs, systems integrators) as their PMIS specialist
* **Leverage LA Metro Success:** Proven Oracle Unifier expertise on multi-billion dollar program is primary competitive advantage
* **Scalable Delivery:** Multi-year implementations across partner portfolio; recurring revenue model
* **Target Partners:** 19 firms across 5 tiers (HNTB, WSP, ARCADIS, HDR as Tier 1 priorities)
* **Financial Model:** $700K+ annual revenue through subcontracts averaging $200K+ each
* **AI-Enabled Operations:** 6-agent suite supports partnership development, proposal response, and recruiting
