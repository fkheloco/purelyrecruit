## Diversity & Inclusion

### Women in Leadership & Workforce

**Distinctive Differentiator:** Women comprise 40%+ of CM Solutions' workforce—approximately **four times the construction industry average** of ~10%.

This is not just a demographic statistic; it's a strategic advantage:

- **Diversity of Thought:** Women leaders and team members bring different perspectives to complex problem-solving
- **Client Appeal:** Many prime contractors and owners have diversity requirements or preferences. CMS's workforce composition is a natural fit
- **WBE Certification Value:** The high percentage of women in leadership and technical roles strengthens WBE certification credentials
- **Talent Pipeline:** CMS actively recruits and develops women in construction, addressing industry-wide talent shortages
- **Culture & Retention:** Inclusive environment contributes to strong employee retention and engagement

### Certifications as Diversity Credentials

- **WBE (Women-Owned Enterprise):** Owned and controlled by women; opens government contracting, prime contractor diversity programs, and MWDBE supplier lists
- **SBE (Small Business Enterprise):** Supports small business support programs at federal, state, and local levels
- **DBE (Disadvantaged Business Enterprise):** Federal program supporting disadvantaged business ownership; applicable to federal projects and some state/local work

These certifications are not just credentials—they're competitive advantages in a marketplace increasingly focused on supplier diversity.
