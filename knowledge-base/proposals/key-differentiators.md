# Purely Works Key Differentiators

## For Use in Proposals, SOQs, and Sales Materials

### 1. True Ownership & Control

**What This Means:**
* Clients own 100% of all code, data, and intellectual property from the moment of creation
* No licensing fees, no shared ownership, no future charges for using what they paid us to build
* True "work for hire" under U.S. copyright law

**Why It Matters:**
* No vendor lock-in
* Complete freedom to modify, extend, or transfer work
* No ongoing dependencies on our systems
* Full control over their competitive advantages

**Use in Proposals:**
"All deliverables become your property immediately upon creation, with no licensing fees or shared ownership. You maintain complete control."
