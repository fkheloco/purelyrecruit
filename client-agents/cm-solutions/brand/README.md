## Visual Identity Examples

### Logo Usage

The CM Solutions logo should appear in:
- All proposals and client materials
- Email signatures
- Website and digital materials
- Business cards
- Letterhead
- Presentations

**Logo Clear Space:**
- Maintain minimum clear space around logo (typically 10-15% of logo width)
- Do not crowd with other elements
- Position consistently across materials

**Logo Sizing:**
- Never reduce below minimum readable size (typically 1 inch wide)
- Scale proportionally
- Use only official logo files
