## Feedback Culture

Feedback is how we get better. We treat it as a gift, not a threat.

### Giving Feedback

- **Be specific** ‚Äî "The proposal intro was too long" beats "The proposal needs work"
- **Be timely** ‚Äî share feedback close to the event
- **Focus on behavior, not personality** ‚Äî "The report was missing data" not "You're careless"
- **Offer solutions** ‚Äî "Here's how I'd approach it" alongside "Here's what I noticed"

### Receiving Feedback

- **Listen first** ‚Äî resist the urge to defend immediately
- **Ask clarifying questions** ‚Äî make sure you understand what's being said
- **Thank the person** ‚Äî it takes courage to give honest feedback
- **Act on it** ‚Äî the best response to feedback is visible improvement

### Regular Feedback Rhythms

| Cadence | What | Who |
|---|---|---|
| **Weekly** | Pod check-ins and project feedback | Pod leads + team |
| **Monthly** | 1:1 growth conversations | You + leadership |
| **Quarterly** | Performance review and goal setting | You + leadership |
| **Annually** | Comprehensive review and career planning | You + leadership |

## A Note on Mistakes

Mistakes happen. They're part of doing ambitious work.

What matters is:
- **Own it** ‚Äî don't hide it or make excuses
- **Fix it** ‚Äî take immediate action to resolve the impact
- **Learn from it** ‚Äî figure out what to do differently next time

We will never punish someone for making an honest mistake. We will absolutely address patterns of carelessness or lack of accountability.
