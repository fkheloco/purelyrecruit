## Cathedral Center - New Platform Product

### Product Overview

**Cathedral Center** is SageCrest's AI-powered assessment and growth enablement platform for financial advisors and advisory practices.

### Five Core Pillars Architecture

Cathedral Center is built on five interconnected pillars providing comprehensive growth intelligence, assessment, automation, enablement, and advisory services:

1. **Growth Intelligence Engine** — Data-driven lead identification and scoring for organic/inorganic growth opportunities; HubSpot-first integration with predictive analytics
2. **Advisory Assessment & Attraction Tools** — Retail client and advisor assessments; three-tier evaluation system (Tier 1 automated, Tier 2 self-assessment, Tier 3 expert interviews); DNA compatibility scoring
3. **Workflow Automation** — Lead nurturing, recruiting/M&A process facilitation, advisor transition workflows, periodic lead re-ranking; HubSpot sequences + AI suggestions
4. **Executive Enablement** — C-suite mastermind sessions, executive coaching, valuation/monetization/exit planning, succession and equity planning
5. **Human + AI Services Layer** — Strategic planning & execution coaching, outsourced Chief AI Officer function, tech stack consulting, client experience transformation, marketing & growth strategy, recruiting & talent development, succession planning

**See:** `/knowledge-base/product/five-pillars.md` for comprehensive architecture documentation

### Platform Architecture

#### Three-Tier Assessment System

**Tier 1: Public Data Enrichment (Automated)**

Fully automated data collection and enrichment using public sources.

**Data Collected:**
- Firm details (location, founding date, leadership)
- Assets Under Management (AUM)
- SEC ADV (Form ADV) filings and compliance history
- Regulatory history and any violations
- Technology stack and systems analysis
- Client base demographics
- Service offerings and specializations

**Scoring:** Automated compatibility scoring based on public signals

**Timeline:** 24-48 hours (fully automated, no advisor input required)

**Use Case:** Initial rapid screening and baseline compatibility assessment

---

**Tier 2: Structured Questionnaires (Self-Administered)**

Comprehensive self-assessment questionnaires covering operational and strategic dimensions.

**Operations Module (~30+ inputs)**
- Staffing and team structure
- Current technology systems and infrastructure
- Compliance and regulatory procedures
- Operational workflows and processes
- Growth processes and capacity
- Client service model
- Fee structure and compensation philosophy
- Performance metrics and KPIs

**Strategy Module**
- Business vision and goals (3-5 years)
- Growth targets (organic vs. inorganic)
- Target market and service focus
- Competitive positioning
- Market opportunities and threats
- Growth strategy execution plans
- Key success factors

**Behaviors Module (DAAN Framework)**
- **D**river behavioral type (competitive, results-oriented, direct)
- **A**nalytical behavioral type (systematic, detail-focused, risk-aware)
- **A**miable behavioral type (relationship-focused, collaborative, supportive)
- **E**xpressive behavioral type (visionary, big-picture, influence-focused)

Identifies behavioral patterns and team dynamics

**Culture Module (~91 questions)**
- Core values and beliefs
- Decision-making style
- Communication preferences
- Work environment preferences
- Growth and ambition orientation
- Client focus vs. operational focus
- Compensation and incentive philosophy
- Leadership style expectations
- Team collaboration expectations
- Innovation and risk tolerance

**Completion Time:** 30-45 minutes for average advisor

**Output:** Self-assessed profile with initial compatibility signals and DNA score preview

---

**Tier 3: Deep Dive Interviews (Human-Led)**

In-depth expert interviews conducted by SageCrest leadership or trained Cathedral specialists.

**Financial Due Diligence**
- Revenue analysis and breakdown by service
- Margin analysis and profitability
- Growth trajectory (3-5 years historical)
- Client concentration and retention
- Asset growth rates
- Compensation structures and costs
- Financial obligations and debt
- Earnings sustainability

**Legal & Compliance Deep Dive**
- Regulatory history and any violations
- Pending investigations or complaints
- Litigation history or exposure
- Compliance infrastructure and procedures
- Staff compliance training
- Technology security and data protection
- Insurance coverage and gaps
- Affiliate or related party relationships

**Integration Planning**
- Operational integration points
- Technology systems integration requirements
- Client communication and transition
- Team structure and compensation alignment
- Service offering integration
- Back-office and operational integration
- Licensing and regulatory transition
- Estimated integration timeline and costs

**Cultural & Fit Assessment**
- Leadership philosophy and approach
- Core values and priorities
- Growth appetite and vision
- Client service philosophy
- Compensation philosophy alignment
- Work culture and team dynamics
- Decision-making authority
- Change management approach

**Timeline:** 2-4 hours (conducted by SageCrest experts)

**Output:** Comprehensive compatibility assessment with detailed DNA score, integration roadmap, and strategic recommendations

### Cathedral Center Assessment Suite

Cathedral Center provides five integrated assessment tools serving different growth scenarios:

#### 1. Advisor Compatibility Index (MVP - Phase 2)
Evaluates cultural, operational, and behavioral fit for recruiting/M&A decisions. Three-tier system: Tier 1 (automated public data), Tier 2 (self-assessment questionnaire), Tier 3 (expert interviews).

#### 2. Firm Growth Readiness Model (Phase 3)
Measures advisory firm preparedness and organizational readiness for growth across strategic clarity, financial capacity, operational infrastructure, team capability, cultural alignment, leadership competency, market position, and financial resources.

#### 3. Client Journey Mapping Tool (Phase 3)
Identifies gaps and improvement opportunities in client experience journey from discovery through ongoing service. Provides detailed experience maps and enhancement roadmaps.

#### 4. Value Accretion Framework (Phase 3)
Quantifies firm and advisor value drivers—identifying which capabilities, operational improvements, and growth initiatives create the most value. Financial impact modeling included.

#### 5. Succession & Equity Participation Assessment (Phase 3)
Plans long-term ownership transition, equity participation, and leadership succession. Includes succession timeline, equity structure, participation plan, leadership development, and exit scenario modeling.

**See:** `/knowledge-base/assessments/assessment-suite.md` for detailed assessment documentation

### Data Entities Model

Cathedral Center manages the following core data entities:

**Tenant** — Organization using Cathedral (SageCrest for MVP)

**Lead** — Prospect advisor, firm, or client prospect with scoring data

**Assessment** — Instance of assessment (Tier 1, 2, 3) for specific lead

**Question** — Assessment question with module categorization and scoring logic

**Response** — Advisor's answer to specific assessment question

**Assessment Result** — Calculated scores and insights from completed assessment

**HubSpot Sync Log** — Tracks bidirectional data synchronization with HubSpot CRM
